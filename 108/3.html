108:3

إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ ٱلۡأَبۡتَرُ

3. For he who hates you (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم), he will be cut off (from posterity and every good thing in this world and in the Hereafter).[2]

[2] (V.108:3) Narrated Anas رضي الله عنه : The Prophet صلى الله عليه وسلم said, "None of you will have Faith till he loves me more than his father, his children and all mankind. (Sahih Al-Bukhari, Vol.1, Hadith No.14).
