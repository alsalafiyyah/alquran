43:89

فَٱصۡفَحۡ عَنۡهُمۡ وَقُلۡ سَلَٰمٞۚ فَسَوۡفَ يَعۡلَمُونَ

89. So turn away from them (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه و سلم), and say: Salâm (peace)! But they will come to know.[6]

[6] (V.43:89) The provision of this Verse was abrogated by the revelation of the Verse of fighting them (V.9.5).
