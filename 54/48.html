54:48

يَوۡمَ يُسۡحَبُونَ فِي ٱلنَّارِ عَلَىٰ وُجُوهِهِمۡ ذُوقُواْ مَسَّ سَقَرَ

48. The Day they will be dragged on their faces into the Fire (it will be said to them): "Taste you the touch of Hell!"[2]

[2] (V.54:48) See (V.25:34) and the footnote of (V.17:97).
