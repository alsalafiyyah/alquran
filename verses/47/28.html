Muhammad

(Verse 28)

Muhammad (47:28)

ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمُ ٱتَّبَعُواْ مَآ أَسۡخَطَ ٱللَّهَ وَكَرِهُواْ رِضۡوَٰنَهُۥ فَأَحۡبَطَ أَعۡمَٰلَهُمۡ

28. That is because they followed that which angered Allâh and hated that which pleased Him. So He made their deeds fruitless.
