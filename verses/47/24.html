Muhammad

(Verse 24)

Muhammad (47:24)

أَفَلَا يَتَدَبَّرُونَ ٱلۡقُرۡءَانَ أَمۡ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبٍ أَقۡفَالُهَآ

24. Do they not then think deeply in the Qur’ân, or are their hearts locked up (from understanding it)?
