Al-Waqi'ah

(96 Verses)

Page 2 of 4

56:31

وَمَآءٖ مَّسۡكُوبٖ ۝31

And by water flowing constantly,

56:32

وَفَٰكِهَةٖ كَثِيرَةٖ ۝32

And fruit in plenty,

56:33

لَّا مَقۡطُوعَةٖ وَلَا مَمۡنُوعَةٖ ۝33

Whose supply is not cut off (by change of season), nor are they out of reach,

56:34

وَفُرُشٖ مَّرۡفُوعَةٍ ۝34

And on couches or thrones, raised high.

56:35

إِنَّآ أَنشَأۡنَٰهُنَّ إِنشَآءٗ ۝35

Verily, We have created them (maidens) of special creation.

56:36

فَجَعَلۡنَٰهُنَّ أَبۡكَارًا ۝36

And made them virgins.

56:37

عُرُبًا أَتۡرَابٗا ۝37

Loving (their husbands only), (and) of equal age.

56:38

لِّأَصۡحَٰبِ ٱلۡيَمِينِ ۝38

For those on the Right Hand.

56:39

ثُلَّةٞ مِّنَ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ ۝39

A multitude of those (on the Right Hand) will be from the first generation (who embraced Islâm).

56:40

وَثُلَّةٞ مِّنَ ٱلۡأٓخِرِينَ ۝40

And a multitude of those (on the Right Hand) will be from the later generations.

56:41

وَأَصۡحَٰبُ ٱلشِّمَالِ مَآ أَصۡحَٰبُ ٱلشِّمَالِ ۝41

And those on the Left Hand - how (unfortunate) will be those on the Left Hand?

56:42

فِي سَمُومٖ وَحَمِيمٖ ۝42

In fierce hot wind and boiling water,

56:43

وَظِلّٖ مِّن يَحۡمُومٖ ۝43

And shadow of black smoke,

56:44

لَّا بَارِدٖ وَلَا كَرِيمٍ ۝44

(That shadow) neither cool, nor (even) pleasant,

56:45

إِنَّهُمۡ كَانُواْ قَبۡلَ ذَٰلِكَ مُتۡرَفِينَ ۝45

Verily, before that, they indulged in luxury,

56:46

وَكَانُواْ يُصِرُّونَ عَلَى ٱلۡحِنثِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ ۝46

And were persisting in great sin (joining partners in worship along with Allâh, committing murder and other crimes)

Footnote — Verse 56:46

[2] (V.56:46) See the footnote (B) of (V.3:130).
56:47

وَكَانُواْ يَقُولُونَ أَئِذَا مِتۡنَا وَكُنَّا تُرَابٗا وَعِظَٰمًا أَءِنَّا لَمَبۡعُوثُونَ ۝47

And they used to say: "When we die and become dust and bones, shall we then indeed be resurrected?

56:48

أَوَءَابَآؤُنَا ٱلۡأَوَّلُونَ ۝48

"And also our forefathers?"

56:49

قُلۡ إِنَّ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ وَٱلۡأٓخِرِينَ ۝49

Say (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم): "(Yes) verily, those of old, and those of later times.

56:50

لَمَجۡمُوعُونَ إِلَىٰ مِيقَٰتِ يَوۡمٖ مَّعۡلُومٖ ۝50

"All will surely be gathered together for appointed Meeting of a known Day.

56:51

ثُمَّ إِنَّكُمۡ أَيُّهَا ٱلضَّآلُّونَ ٱلۡمُكَذِّبُونَ ۝51

"Then moreover, verily you the erring-ones, the deniers (of Resurrection)!

56:52

لَأٓكِلُونَ مِن شَجَرٖ مِّن زَقُّومٖ ۝52

"You verily will eat of the trees of Zaqqûm.

56:53

فَمَالِـُٔونَ مِنۡهَا ٱلۡبُطُونَ ۝53

"Then you will fill your bellies therewith,

56:54

فَشَٰرِبُونَ عَلَيۡهِ مِنَ ٱلۡحَمِيمِ ۝54

"And drink boiling water on top of it.

56:55

فَشَٰرِبُونَ شُرۡبَ ٱلۡهِيمِ ۝55

"And you will drink (that) like thirsty camels!"

56:56

هَٰذَا نُزُلُهُمۡ يَوۡمَ ٱلدِّينِ ۝56

That will be their entertainment on the Day of Recompense!

56:57

نَحۡنُ خَلَقۡنَٰكُمۡ فَلَوۡلَا تُصَدِّقُونَ ۝57

We created you: then why do you believe not?

56:58

أَفَرَءَيۡتُم مَّا تُمۡنُونَ ۝58

Then tell Me (about) the (human) semen that you emit.

56:59

ءَأَنتُمۡ تَخۡلُقُونَهُۥٓ أَمۡ نَحۡنُ ٱلۡخَٰلِقُونَ ۝59

Is it you who create it (i.e. make this semen into a perfect human being), or are We the Creator?

56:60

نَحۡنُ قَدَّرۡنَا بَيۡنَكُمُ ٱلۡمَوۡتَ وَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمَسۡبُوقِينَ ۝60

We have decreed death to you all, and We are not outstripped,
