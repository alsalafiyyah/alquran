(Verse 10)

Al-Hajj (22:10)

ذَٰلِكَ بِمَا قَدَّمَتۡ يَدَاكَ وَأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَيۡسَ بِظَلَّٰمٖ لِّلۡعَبِيدِ

10. That is because of what your hands have sent forth, and verily, Allâh is not unjust to (His) slaves.
