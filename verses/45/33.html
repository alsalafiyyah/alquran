Al-Jathiyah

(Verse 33)

Al-Jathiyah (45:33)

وَبَدَا لَهُمۡ سَيِّـَٔاتُ مَا عَمِلُواْ وَحَاقَ بِهِم مَّا كَانُواْ بِهِۦ يَسۡتَهۡزِءُونَ

And the evil of what they did will appear to them, and that which they used to mock at will completely encircle them.
