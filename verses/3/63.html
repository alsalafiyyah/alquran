(Verse 63)

Aal 'Imran (3:63)

فَإِن تَوَلَّوۡاْ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ عَلِيمُۢ بِٱلۡمُفۡسِدِينَ

And if they turn away (and do not accept these true proofs and evidence), then surely, Allâh is All-Aware of those who do mischief.
