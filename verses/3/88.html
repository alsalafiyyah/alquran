(Verse 88)

Aal 'Imran (3:88)

خَٰلِدِينَ فِيهَا لَا يُخَفَّفُ عَنۡهُمُ ٱلۡعَذَابُ وَلَا هُمۡ يُنظَرُونَ

88. They will abide therein (Hell). Neither will their torment be lightened, nor will it be delayed or postponed (for a while).
