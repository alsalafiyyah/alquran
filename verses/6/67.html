(Verse 67)

Al-An'am (6:67)

لِّكُلِّ نَبَإٖ مُّسۡتَقَرّٞۚ وَسَوۡفَ تَعۡلَمُونَ

67. For every news there is a reality[7] and you will come to know.

[7] (V.6:67): i.e. for everything there is an appointed term, and for every deed there is a recompense.
