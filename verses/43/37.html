(Verse 37)

Az-Zukhruf (43:37)

وَإِنَّهُمۡ لَيَصُدُّونَهُمۡ عَنِ ٱلسَّبِيلِ وَيَحۡسَبُونَ أَنَّهُم مُّهۡتَدُونَ

And verily, they (Satans / devils) hinder them from the Path (of Allâh), but they think that they are guided aright!
