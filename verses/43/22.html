Az-Zukhruf

(Verse 22)

Az-Zukhruf (43:22)

بَلۡ قَالُوٓاْ إِنَّا وَجَدۡنَآ ءَابَآءَنَا عَلَىٰٓ أُمَّةٖ وَإِنَّا عَلَىٰٓ ءَاثَٰرِهِم مُّهۡتَدُونَ

Nay! They say: "We found our fathers following a certain way and religion, and we guide ourselves by their footsteps."
