(Verse 25)

Az-Zukhruf (43:25)

فَٱنتَقَمۡنَا مِنۡهُمۡۖ فَٱنظُرۡ كَيۡفَ كَانَ عَٰقِبَةُ ٱلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ

25. So We took revenge on them, then see what was the end of those who denied (Islâmic Monotheism).
