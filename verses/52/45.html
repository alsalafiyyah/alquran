At-Tur (52:45)

فَذَرۡهُمۡ حَتَّىٰ يُلَٰقُواْ يَوۡمَهُمُ ٱلَّذِي فِيهِ يُصۡعَقُونَ

45. So leave them alone till they meet their Day, in which they will sink into a fainting (with horror).
