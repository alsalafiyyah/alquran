(Verse 49)

Yusuf (12:49)

ثُمَّ يَأۡتِي مِنۢ بَعۡدِ ذَٰلِكَ عَامٞ فِيهِ يُغَاثُ ٱلنَّاسُ وَفِيهِ يَعۡصِرُونَ

49. "Then thereafter will come a year in which people will have abundant rain and in which they will press (wine and oil)."
