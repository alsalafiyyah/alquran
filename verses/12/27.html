(Verse 27)

Yusuf (12:27)

وَإِن كَانَ قَمِيصُهُۥ قُدَّ مِن دُبُرٖ فَكَذَبَتۡ وَهُوَ مِنَ ٱلصَّٰدِقِينَ

27. "But if it be that his shirt is torn from the back, then she has told a lie and he is speaking the truth!"
