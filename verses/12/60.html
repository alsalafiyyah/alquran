(Verse 60)

Yusuf (12:60)

فَإِن لَّمۡ تَأۡتُونِي بِهِۦ فَلَا كَيۡلَ لَكُمۡ عِندِي وَلَا تَقۡرَبُونِ

60. "But if you bring him not to me, there shall be no measure (of corn) for you with me, nor shall you come near me."
