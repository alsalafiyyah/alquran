(Verse 95)

Hud (11:95)

كَأَن لَّمۡ يَغۡنَوۡاْ فِيهَآۗ أَلَا بُعۡدٗا لِّمَدۡيَنَ كَمَا بَعِدَتۡ ثَمُودُ

As if they had never lived there! So away with Madyan (Midian) as away with Thamûd! (All these nations were destroyed).
