11:72

قَالَتۡ يَٰوَيۡلَتَىٰٓ ءَأَلِدُ وَأَنَا۠ عَجُوزٞ وَهَٰذَا بَعۡلِي شَيۡخًاۖ إِنَّ هَٰذَا لَشَيۡءٌ عَجِيبٞ

72. She said (in astonishment): "Woe unto me! Shall I bear a child while I am an old woman,[5] and here is my husband an old man? Verily! This is a strange thing!"

[5] (V.11:72) See (V.51:29).
