Surah 104

Verse 104:1 Focus Verse →

وَيۡلٞ لِّكُلِّ هُمَزَةٖ لُّمَزَةٍ

1. Woe to every slanderer and backbiter.[1]

Verse 104:2 Focus Verse →

ٱلَّذِي جَمَعَ مَالٗا وَعَدَّدَهُۥ

2. Who has gathered wealth and counted it.

Verse 104:3 Focus Verse →

يَحۡسَبُ أَنَّ مَالَهُۥٓ أَخۡلَدَهُۥ

3. He thinks that his wealth will make him last forever!

Verse 104:4 Focus Verse →

كَلَّاۖ لَيُنۢبَذَنَّ فِي ٱلۡحُطَمَةِ

4. Nay! Verily, he will be thrown into the crushing Fire.

Verse 104:5 Focus Verse →

وَمَآ أَدۡرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلۡحُطَمَةُ

5. And what will make you know what the crushing Fire is?

Verse 104:6 Focus Verse →

نَارُ ٱللَّهِ ٱلۡمُوقَدَةُ

6. The fire of Allâh kindled,

Verse 104:7 Focus Verse →

ٱلَّتِي تَطَّلِعُ عَلَى ٱلۡأَفۡـِٔدَةِ

7. Which leaps up over the hearts,

Verse 104:8 Focus Verse →

إِنَّهَا عَلَيۡهِم مُّؤۡصَدَةٞ

8. Verily, it shall be closed upon them,

Verse 104:9 Focus Verse →

فِي عَمَدٖ مُّمَدَّدَةِۭ

9. In pillars stretched forth (i.e. they will be punished in the Fire with pillars).
