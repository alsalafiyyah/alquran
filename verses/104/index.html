Al-Humazah

(9 Verses)

104:1

وَيۡلٞ لِّكُلِّ هُمَزَةٖ لُّمَزَةٍ 1

Woe to every slanderer and backbiter.

Footnote — Verse 104:1

[1] (V.104:1) See the footnotes A, B, C, of (V.49:12).
104:2

ٱلَّذِي جَمَعَ مَالٗا وَعَدَّدَهُۥ 2

Who has gathered wealth and counted it.

104:3

يَحۡسَبُ أَنَّ مَالَهُۥٓ أَخۡلَدَهُۥ 3

He thinks that his wealth will make him last forever!

104:4

كَلَّاۖ لَيُنۢبَذَنَّ فِي ٱلۡحُطَمَةِ 4

Nay! Verily, he will be thrown into the crushing Fire.

104:5

وَمَآ أَدۡرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلۡحُطَمَةُ 5

And what will make you know what the crushing Fire is?

104:6

نَارُ ٱللَّهِ ٱلۡمُوقَدَةُ 6

The fire of Allâh kindled,

104:7

ٱلَّتِي تَطَّلِعُ عَلَى ٱلۡأَفۡـِٔدَةِ 7

Which leaps up over the hearts,

104:8

إِنَّهَا عَلَيۡهِم مُّؤۡصَدَةٞ 8

Verily, it shall be closed upon them,

104:9

فِي عَمَدٖ مُّمَدَّدَةِۭ 9

In pillars stretched forth (i.e. they will be punished in the Fire with pillars).
