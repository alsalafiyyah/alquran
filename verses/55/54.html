(Verse 54)

Ar-Rahman (55:54)

مُتَّكِـِٔينَ عَلَىٰ فُرُشِۭ بَطَآئِنُهَا مِنۡ إِسۡتَبۡرَقٖۚ وَجَنَى ٱلۡجَنَّتَيۡنِ دَانٖ

54. Reclining upon the couches lined with silk brocade, and the fruits of the two Gardens will be near at hand.
