Ar-Rahman

(78 Verses)

Page 3 of 3

55:61

فَبِأَيِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinn and men) deny?

55:62

وَمِن دُونِهِمَا جَنَّتَانِ

And besides these two, there are two other Gardens (i.e. in Paradise).

55:63

فَبِأَيِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinn and men) deny?

55:64

مُدۡهَآمَّتَانِ

Dark green (in colour).

55:65

فَبِأَيِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinn and men) deny?

55:66

فِيهِمَا عَيۡنَانِ نَضَّاخَتَانِ

In them (both) will be two springs gushing forth.

55:67

فَبِأَيِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinn and men) deny?

55:68

فِيهِمَا فَٰكِهَةٞ وَنَخۡلٞ وَرُمَّانٞ

In them (both) will be fruits, and date-palms and pomegranates.

55:69

فَبِأَيِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinn and men) deny?

55:70

فِيهِنَّ خَيۡرَٰتٌ حِسَانٞ

Therein (Gardens) will be Khairâtun-Hisân [fair (wives) good and beautiful];

55:71

فَبِأَيِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinn and men) deny?

55:72

حُورٞ مَّقۡصُورَٰتٞ فِي ٱلۡخِيَامِ

Hûr (beautiful, fair females) guarded in pavilions;

Footnote — Verse 55:72

[4] (V.55:72) See footnote of (V.52:20).
55:73

فَبِأَيِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinn and men) deny?

55:74

لَمۡ يَطۡمِثۡهُنَّ إِنسٞ قَبۡلَهُمۡ وَلَا جَآنّٞ

With whom no man or jinni has had tamth before them.

Footnote — Verse 55:74

[5] (V.55:74) Tamth means: Opening their hymens with sexual intercourse.
55:75

فَبِأَيِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinn and men) deny?

55:76

مُتَّكِـِٔينَ عَلَىٰ رَفۡرَفٍ خُضۡرٖ وَعَبۡقَرِيٍّ حِسَانٖ

Reclining on green cushions and rich beautiful mattresses.

55:77

فَبِأَيِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ

Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinn and men) deny?

55:78

تَبَٰرَكَ ٱسۡمُ رَبِّكَ ذِي ٱلۡجَلَٰلِ وَٱلۡإِكۡرَامِ

Blessed be the Name of your Lord (Allâh), the Owner of Majesty and Honour.
