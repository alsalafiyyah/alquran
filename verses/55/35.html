Ar-Rahman

(Verse 35)

Ar-Rahman (55:35)

يُرۡسَلُ عَلَيۡكُمَا شُوَاظٞ مِّن نَّارٖ وَنُحَاسٞ فَلَا تَنتَصِرَانِ

35. There will be sent against you both, smokeless flames of fire and (molten) brass, and you will not be able to defend yourselves.
