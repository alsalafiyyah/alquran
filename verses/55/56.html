(Verse 56)

Ar-Rahman (55:56)

فِيهِنَّ قَٰصِرَٰتُ ٱلطَّرۡفِ لَمۡ يَطۡمِثۡهُنَّ إِنسٞ قَبۡلَهُمۡ وَلَا جَآنّٞ

Wherein both will be Qâsirât-ut-Tarf [chaste females (wives) restraining their glances, desiring none except their husbands], with whom no man or jinni has had tamth before them.

Footnote — Verse 55:56

[3] (V.55:56): Tamth means: Opening their hymens with sexual intercourse.
