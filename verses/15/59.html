(Verse 59)

Al-Hijr (15:59)

إِلَّآ ءَالَ لُوطٍ إِنَّا لَمُنَجُّوهُمۡ أَجۡمَعِينَ

59. "(All) except the family of Lût (Lot). Them all we are surely going to save (from destruction).
