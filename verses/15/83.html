Al-Hijr (15:83)

فَأَخَذَتۡهُمُ ٱلصَّيۡحَةُ مُصۡبِحِينَ

83. But As-Saihah (torment - awful cry) overtook them in the early morning (of the fourth day of their promised punishment days).
