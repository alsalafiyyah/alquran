(Verse 74)

Al-Hijr (15:74)

فَجَعَلۡنَا عَٰلِيَهَا سَافِلَهَا وَأَمۡطَرۡنَا عَلَيۡهِمۡ حِجَارَةٗ مِّن سِجِّيلٍ

74. And We turned (the towns of Sodom in Palestine) upside down and rained down on them stones of baked clay.
