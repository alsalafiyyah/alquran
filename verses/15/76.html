15:76

وَإِنَّهَا لَبِسَبِيلٖ مُّقِيمٍ

76. And verily! They (the cities) were right on the highroad (from Makkah to Syria i.e. the place where the Dead Sea is now)[5].

[5] (V.15:76) Please see the Book of History by Ibn Kathir (كتاب التاريخ لابن كثير).
