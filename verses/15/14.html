(Verse 14)

Al-Hijr (15:14)

وَلَوۡ فَتَحۡنَا عَلَيۡهِم بَابٗا مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ فَظَلُّواْ فِيهِ يَعۡرُجُونَ

And even if We opened to them a gate from the heaven and they were to keep on ascending thereto (all the day long)
