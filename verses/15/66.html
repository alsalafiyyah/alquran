(Verse 66)

Al-Hijr (15:66)

وَقَضَيۡنَآ إِلَيۡهِ ذَٰلِكَ ٱلۡأَمۡرَ أَنَّ دَابِرَ هَٰٓؤُلَآءِ مَقۡطُوعٞ مُّصۡبِحِينَ

66. And We made known this decree to him, that the root of those (sinners) was to be cut off in the early morning.
