(Verse 61)

Ya-Sin (36:61)

وَأَنِ ٱعۡبُدُونِيۚ هَٰذَا صِرَٰطٞ مُّسۡتَقِيمٞ

61. And that you should worship Me [Alone - Islâmic Monotheism, and set up not rivals, associate-gods with Me]. That is the Straight Path.[3]

[3] (V.36:61) See the footnote of (V.2:22).
