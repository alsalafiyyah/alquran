Ya-Sin (36:72)

وَذَلَّلۡنَٰهَا لَهُمۡ فَمِنۡهَا رَكُوبُهُمۡ وَمِنۡهَا يَأۡكُلُونَ

72. And We have subdued them unto them so that some of them they have for riding and some they eat.
