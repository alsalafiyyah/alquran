Al-Ahzab (33:57)

إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يُؤۡذُونَ ٱللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُۥ لَعَنَهُمُ ٱللَّهُ فِي ٱلدُّنۡيَا وَٱلۡأٓخِرَةِ وَأَعَدَّ لَهُمۡ عَذَابٗا مُّهِينٗا

57. Verily, those who annoy Allâh and His Messenger (صلى الله عليه وسلم)[12] Allâh has cursed them in this world, and in the Hereafter, and has prepared for them a humiliating torment.

[12] (V.33:57) A) By abusing or telling lies against Allâh and His Messenger صلى الله عليه وسلم, by making pictures - imitating Allâh’s creation, and by disobeying Allâh and His Messengerصلى الله عليه وسلم. B) See the footnote of (V.2:278).
