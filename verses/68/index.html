Surah 68

Page 1 of 2

Verse 68:1 Focus Verse →

نٓۚ وَٱلۡقَلَمِ وَمَا يَسۡطُرُونَ

1. Nûn. [These letters (Nun, etc.) are one of the miracles of the Qur’an, and none but Allah (Alone) knows their meanings]. By the pen and by what they (the angels) write (in the Records of men).

Verse 68:2 Focus Verse →

مَآ أَنتَ بِنِعۡمَةِ رَبِّكَ بِمَجۡنُونٖ

2. You (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم), by the Grace of your Lord, are not mad.

Verse 68:3 Focus Verse →

وَإِنَّ لَكَ لَأَجۡرًا غَيۡرَ مَمۡنُونٖ

3. And Verily, for you (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) will be an endless reward.

Verse 68:4 Focus Verse →

وَإِنَّكَ لَعَلَىٰ خُلُقٍ عَظِيمٖ

4. And Verily, you (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) are on an exalted (standard of) character.

Verse 68:5 Focus Verse →

فَسَتُبۡصِرُ وَيُبۡصِرُونَ

5. You will see, and they will see,

Verse 68:6 Focus Verse →

بِأَييِّكُمُ ٱلۡمَفۡتُونُ

6. Which of you is afflicted with madness.

Verse 68:7 Focus Verse →

إِنَّ رَبَّكَ هُوَ أَعۡلَمُ بِمَن ضَلَّ عَن سَبِيلِهِۦ وَهُوَ أَعۡلَمُ بِٱلۡمُهۡتَدِينَ

7. Verily, your Lord is the Best Knower of him who has gone astray from His Path, and He is the Best Knower of those who are guided.

Verse 68:8 Focus Verse →

فَلَا تُطِعِ ٱلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ

8. So (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) obey you not the deniers [(of Islâmic Monotheism - those who belie the Verses of Allâh), the Oneness of Allâh, and the Messengership of Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم][1]

Verse 68:9 Focus Verse →

وَدُّواْ لَوۡ تُدۡهِنُ فَيُدۡهِنُونَ

9. They wish that you should compromise (in religion out of courtesy) with them: so they (too) would compromise with you.

Verse 68:10 Focus Verse →

وَلَا تُطِعۡ كُلَّ حَلَّافٖ مَّهِينٍ

10. And (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) obey you not everyone Hallaf Mahîn (the one who swears much and is a liar or is worthless). [Tafsir At-Tabari]

Verse 68:11 Focus Verse →

هَمَّازٖ مَّشَّآءِۭ بِنَمِيمٖ

11. A slanderer, going about with calumnies,

Verse 68:12 Focus Verse →

مَّنَّاعٖ لِّلۡخَيۡرِ مُعۡتَدٍ أَثِيمٍ

12. Hinderer of the good, transgressor, sinful,

Verse 68:13 Focus Verse →

عُتُلِّۭ بَعۡدَ ذَٰلِكَ زَنِيمٍ

13. Cruel, and moreover base-born (of illegitimate birth).

Verse 68:14 Focus Verse →

أَن كَانَ ذَا مَالٖ وَبَنِينَ

14. (He was so) because he had wealth and children.

Verse 68:15 Focus Verse →

إِذَا تُتۡلَىٰ عَلَيۡهِ ءَايَٰتُنَا قَالَ أَسَٰطِيرُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ

15. When Our Verses (of the Qur’ân) are recited to him, he says: "Tales of the men of old!"

Verse 68:16 Focus Verse →

سَنَسِمُهُۥ عَلَى ٱلۡخُرۡطُومِ

16. We shall brand him on the snout (nose)!

Verse 68:17 Focus Verse →

إِنَّا بَلَوۡنَٰهُمۡ كَمَا بَلَوۡنَآ أَصۡحَٰبَ ٱلۡجَنَّةِ إِذۡ أَقۡسَمُواْ لَيَصۡرِمُنَّهَا مُصۡبِحِينَ

17. Verily, We have tried them as We tried the people of the garden, when they swore to pluck the fruits of the (garden) in the morning,

Verse 68:18 Focus Verse →

وَلَا يَسۡتَثۡنُونَ

18. Without saying: Inshâ’ Allâh (If Allâh wills).

Verse 68:19 Focus Verse →

فَطَافَ عَلَيۡهَا طَآئِفٞ مِّن رَّبِّكَ وَهُمۡ نَآئِمُونَ

19. Then there passed by on the (garden) a visitation (fire) from your Lord at night and burnt it while they were asleep.

Verse 68:20 Focus Verse →

فَأَصۡبَحَتۡ كَٱلصَّرِيمِ

20. So the (garden) became black by the morning, like a pitch dark night (in complete ruins).

Verse 68:21 Focus Verse →

فَتَنَادَوۡاْ مُصۡبِحِينَ

21. Then they called out one to another as soon as the morning broke.

Verse 68:22 Focus Verse →

أَنِ ٱغۡدُواْ عَلَىٰ حَرۡثِكُمۡ إِن كُنتُمۡ صَٰرِمِينَ

22. Saying: "Go to your tilth in the morning, if you would pluck the fruits."

Verse 68:23 Focus Verse →

فَٱنطَلَقُواْ وَهُمۡ يَتَخَٰفَتُونَ

23. So they departed, conversing in secret low tones (saying):

Verse 68:24 Focus Verse →

أَن لَّا يَدۡخُلَنَّهَا ٱلۡيَوۡمَ عَلَيۡكُم مِّسۡكِينٞ

24. "No Miskîn (poor man) shall enter upon you into it today."

Verse 68:25 Focus Verse →

وَغَدَوۡاْ عَلَىٰ حَرۡدٖ قَٰدِرِينَ

25. And they went in the morning with strong intention, thinking that they have power (to prevent the poor taking anything of the fruits therefrom).

Verse 68:26 Focus Verse →

فَلَمَّا رَأَوۡهَا قَالُوٓاْ إِنَّا لَضَآلُّونَ

26. But when they saw the (garden), they said: "Verily, we have gone astray."

Verse 68:27 Focus Verse →

بَلۡ نَحۡنُ مَحۡرُومُونَ

27. (Then they said): "Nay! Indeed we are deprived of (the fruits)!"

Verse 68:28 Focus Verse →

قَالَ أَوۡسَطُهُمۡ أَلَمۡ أَقُل لَّكُمۡ لَوۡلَا تُسَبِّحُونَ

28. The best among them said: "Did I not tell you: why say you not: Inshâ’ Allâh (If Allâh wills)."

Verse 68:29 Focus Verse →

قَالُواْ سُبۡحَٰنَ رَبِّنَآ إِنَّا كُنَّا ظَٰلِمِينَ

29. They said: "Glory to Our Lord! Verily, we have been Zâlimûn (wrong-doers)."

Verse 68:30 Focus Verse →

فَأَقۡبَلَ بَعۡضُهُمۡ عَلَىٰ بَعۡضٖ يَتَلَٰوَمُونَ

30. Then they turned one against another, blaming.
