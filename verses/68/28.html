(Verse 28)

Al-Qalam (68:28)

قَالَ أَوۡسَطُهُمۡ أَلَمۡ أَقُل لَّكُمۡ لَوۡلَا تُسَبِّحُونَ

The best among them said: "Did I not tell you: why say you not: Inshâ’ Allâh (If Allâh wills)."
