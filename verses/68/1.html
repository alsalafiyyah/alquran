Al-Qalam (68:1)

نٓۚ وَٱلۡقَلَمِ وَمَا يَسۡطُرُونَ

1. Nûn. [These letters (Nun, etc.) are one of the miracles of the Qur’an, and none but Allah (Alone) knows their meanings]. By the pen and by what they (the angels) write (in the Records of men).
