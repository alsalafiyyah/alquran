(Verse 3)

Al-Isra' (17:3)

ذُرِّيَّةَ مَنۡ حَمَلۡنَا مَعَ نُوحٍۚ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ عَبۡدٗا شَكُورٗا

"O offspring of those whom We carried (in the ship) with Nûh (Noah)! Verily, he was a grateful slave."
