(Verse 177)

As-Saffat (37:177)

فَإِذَا نَزَلَ بِسَاحَتِهِمۡ فَسَآءَ صَبَاحُ ٱلۡمُنذَرِينَ

Then, when it descends in their courtyard (i.e. near to them), evil will be the morning for those who had been warned!
