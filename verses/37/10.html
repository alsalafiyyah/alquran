As-Saffat

(Verse 10)

As-Saffat (37:10)

إِلَّا مَنۡ خَطِفَ ٱلۡخَطۡفَةَ فَأَتۡبَعَهُۥ شِهَابٞ ثَاقِبٞ

10. Except such as snatch away something by stealing, and they are pursued by a flaming fire of piercing brightness.
