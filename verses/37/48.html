(Verse 48)

As-Saffat (37:48)

وَعِندَهُمۡ قَٰصِرَٰتُ ٱلطَّرۡفِ عِينٞ

48. And beside them will be Qâsirât-at-Tarf [chaste females (wives), restraining their glances (desiring none except their husbands)], with wide and beautiful eyes.[4]

[4] (V.37:48) See the footnote of (V.29:64).
