Surah 37

Page 2 of 7

Verse 37:31 Focus Verse →

فَحَقَّ عَلَيۡنَا قَوۡلُ رَبِّنَآۖ إِنَّا لَذَآئِقُونَ

31. "So now the Word of our Lord has been justified against us, that we shall certainly (have to) taste (the torment).

Verse 37:32 Focus Verse →

فَأَغۡوَيۡنَٰكُمۡ إِنَّا كُنَّا غَٰوِينَ

32. "So we led you astray because we were ourselves astray."

Verse 37:33 Focus Verse →

فَإِنَّهُمۡ يَوۡمَئِذٖ فِي ٱلۡعَذَابِ مُشۡتَرِكُونَ

33. Then verily, that Day, they will (all) share in the torment.

Verse 37:34 Focus Verse →

إِنَّا كَذَٰلِكَ نَفۡعَلُ بِٱلۡمُجۡرِمِينَ

34. Certainly, that is how We deal with Al-Mujrimûn (polytheists, sinners, disbelievers, criminals, the disobedient to Allâh).

Verse 37:35 Focus Verse →

إِنَّهُمۡ كَانُوٓاْ إِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمۡ لَآ إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا ٱللَّهُ يَسۡتَكۡبِرُونَ

35. Truly, when it was said to them: Lâ ilâha illallâh "(none has the right to be worshipped but Allâh)," they puffed themselves up with pride[3] (i.e. denied it).

Verse 37:36 Focus Verse →

وَيَقُولُونَ أَئِنَّا لَتَارِكُوٓاْ ءَالِهَتِنَا لِشَاعِرٖ مَّجۡنُونِۭ

36. And (they) said: "Are we going to abandon our âlihah (gods) for the sake of a mad poet?"

Verse 37:37 Focus Verse →

بَلۡ جَآءَ بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَصَدَّقَ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ

37. Nay! he (Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) has come with the truth (i.e. Allâh’s religion - Islâmic Monotheism and this Qur’ân) and he confirms the Messengers (before him who brought Allâh’s religion - Islâmic Monotheism).

Verse 37:38 Focus Verse →

إِنَّكُمۡ لَذَآئِقُواْ ٱلۡعَذَابِ ٱلۡأَلِيمِ

38. Verily, you (pagans of Makkah) are going to taste the painful torment;

Verse 37:39 Focus Verse →

وَمَا تُجۡزَوۡنَ إِلَّا مَا كُنتُمۡ تَعۡمَلُونَ

39. And you will be requited nothing except for what you used to do (evil deeds, sins, and Allâh’s disobedience which you used to do in this world)

Verse 37:40 Focus Verse →

إِلَّا عِبَادَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلۡمُخۡلَصِينَ

40. Save the chosen slaves of Allâh (i.e. the true believers of Islâmic Monotheism).

Verse 37:41 Focus Verse →

أُوْلَٰٓئِكَ لَهُمۡ رِزۡقٞ مَّعۡلُومٞ

41. For them there will be a known provision (in Paradise),

Verse 37:42 Focus Verse →

فَوَٰكِهُ وَهُم مُّكۡرَمُونَ

42. Fruits; and they shall be honoured,

Verse 37:43 Focus Verse →

فِي جَنَّٰتِ ٱلنَّعِيمِ

43. In the Gardens of delight (Paradise),

Verse 37:44 Focus Verse →

عَلَىٰ سُرُرٖ مُّتَقَٰبِلِينَ

44. Facing one another on thrones.

Verse 37:45 Focus Verse →

يُطَافُ عَلَيۡهِم بِكَأۡسٖ مِّن مَّعِينِۭ

45. Round them will be passed a cup of pure wine -

Verse 37:46 Focus Verse →

بَيۡضَآءَ لَذَّةٖ لِّلشَّٰرِبِينَ

46. White, delicious to the drinkers.

Verse 37:47 Focus Verse →

لَا فِيهَا غَوۡلٞ وَلَا هُمۡ عَنۡهَا يُنزَفُونَ

47. Neither will they have Ghoul (any kind of hurt, abdominal pain, headache, a sin) from that, nor will they suffer intoxication therefrom.

Verse 37:48 Focus Verse →

وَعِندَهُمۡ قَٰصِرَٰتُ ٱلطَّرۡفِ عِينٞ

48. And beside them will be Qâsirât-at-Tarf [chaste females (wives), restraining their glances (desiring none except their husbands)], with wide and beautiful eyes.[4]

Verse 37:49 Focus Verse →

كَأَنَّهُنَّ بَيۡضٞ مَّكۡنُونٞ

49. (Delicate and pure) as if they were (hidden) eggs (well) preserved.

Verse 37:50 Focus Verse →

فَأَقۡبَلَ بَعۡضُهُمۡ عَلَىٰ بَعۡضٖ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ

50. Then they will turn to one another, mutually questioning.

Verse 37:51 Focus Verse →

قَالَ قَآئِلٞ مِّنۡهُمۡ إِنِّي كَانَ لِي قَرِينٞ

51. A speaker of them will say: "Verily, I had a companion (in the world),

Verse 37:52 Focus Verse →

يَقُولُ أَءِنَّكَ لَمِنَ ٱلۡمُصَدِّقِينَ

52. Who used to say: "Are you among those who believe (in resurrection after death).

Verse 37:53 Focus Verse →

أَءِذَا مِتۡنَا وَكُنَّا تُرَابٗا وَعِظَٰمًا أَءِنَّا لَمَدِينُونَ

53. "(That) when we die and become dust and bones, shall we indeed (be raised up) to receive reward or punishment (according to our deeds)?"

Verse 37:54 Focus Verse →

قَالَ هَلۡ أَنتُم مُّطَّلِعُونَ

54. (The speaker) said: "Will you look down?"

Verse 37:55 Focus Verse →

فَٱطَّلَعَ فَرَءَاهُ فِي سَوَآءِ ٱلۡجَحِيمِ

55. So he looked down and saw him in the midst of the Fire.

Verse 37:56 Focus Verse →

قَالَ تَٱللَّهِ إِن كِدتَّ لَتُرۡدِينِ

56. He said: "By Allâh! You have nearly ruined me.

Verse 37:57 Focus Verse →

وَلَوۡلَا نِعۡمَةُ رَبِّي لَكُنتُ مِنَ ٱلۡمُحۡضَرِينَ

57. "Had it not been for the Grace of my Lord, I would certainly have been among those brought forth (to Hell)."

Verse 37:58 Focus Verse →

أَفَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمَيِّتِينَ

58. (The dwellers of Paradise will say) "Are we then not to die (any more)?

Verse 37:59 Focus Verse →

إِلَّا مَوۡتَتَنَا ٱلۡأُولَىٰ وَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمُعَذَّبِينَ

59. Except our first death, and we shall not be punished? (after we have entered Paradise)."

Verse 37:60 Focus Verse →

إِنَّ هَٰذَا لَهُوَ ٱلۡفَوۡزُ ٱلۡعَظِيمُ

60. Truly, this is the supreme success!
