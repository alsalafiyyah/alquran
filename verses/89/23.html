(Verse 23)

Al-Fajr (89:23)

وَجِاْيٓءَ يَوۡمَئِذِۭ بِجَهَنَّمَۚ يَوۡمَئِذٖ يَتَذَكَّرُ ٱلۡإِنسَٰنُ وَأَنَّىٰ لَهُ ٱلذِّكۡرَىٰ

23. And Hell will be brought near that Day. On that Day will man remember, but how will that remembrance (then) avail him?
