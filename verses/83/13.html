Al-Mutaffifin

(Verse 13)

Al-Mutaffifin (83:13)

إِذَا تُتۡلَىٰ عَلَيۡهِ ءَايَٰتُنَا قَالَ أَسَٰطِيرُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ

When Our Verses (of the Qur’ân) are recited to him he says: "Tales of the ancients!"
