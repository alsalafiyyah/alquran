(Verse 19)

Al-Qamar (54:19)

إِنَّآ أَرۡسَلۡنَا عَلَيۡهِمۡ رِيحٗا صَرۡصَرٗا فِي يَوۡمِ نَحۡسٖ مُّسۡتَمِرّٖ

19. Verily, We sent against them a furious wind of harsh voice on a day of evil omen and continuous calamity.
