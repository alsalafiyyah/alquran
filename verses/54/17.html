(Verse 17)

Al-Qamar (54:17)

وَلَقَدۡ يَسَّرۡنَا ٱلۡقُرۡءَانَ لِلذِّكۡرِ فَهَلۡ مِن مُّدَّكِرٖ

17. And We have indeed made the Qur’ân easy to understand and remember; then is there any one who will remember (or receive admonition)?
