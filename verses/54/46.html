Al-Qamar

(Verse 46)

Al-Qamar (54:46)

بَلِ ٱلسَّاعَةُ مَوۡعِدُهُمۡ وَٱلسَّاعَةُ أَدۡهَىٰ وَأَمَرُّ

46. Nay, but the Hour is their appointed time (for their full recompense), and the Hour will be more grievous and more bitter.
