Al-Qamar (54:28)

وَنَبِّئۡهُمۡ أَنَّ ٱلۡمَآءَ قِسۡمَةُۢ بَيۡنَهُمۡۖ كُلُّ شِرۡبٖ مُّحۡتَضَرٞ

28. And inform them that the water is to be shared between (her and) them, each one’s right to drink being established (by turns).
