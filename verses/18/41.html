Al-Kahf (18:41)

أَوۡ يُصۡبِحَ مَآؤُهَا غَوۡرٗا فَلَن تَسۡتَطِيعَ لَهُۥ طَلَبٗا

41. "Or the water thereof (of the gardens) becomes deep-sunken (underground) so that you will never be able to seek it."
