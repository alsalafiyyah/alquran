(Verse 38)

Al-Kahf (18:38)

لَّٰكِنَّا۠ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ رَبِّي وَلَآ أُشۡرِكُ بِرَبِّيٓ أَحَدٗا

38. "But as for my part, (I believe) that He is Allâh, my Lord, and none shall I associate as partner with my Lord.
