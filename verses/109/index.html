Surah 109

Verse 109:1 Focus Verse →

قُلۡ يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلۡكَٰفِرُونَ

1. Say: (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم to these Mushrikûn and Kâfirûn): "O Al-Kafirûn (disbelievers in Allâh, in His Oneness, in His Angels, in His Books, in His Messengers, in the Day of Resurrection, and in Al-Qadar)!

Verse 109:2 Focus Verse →

لَآ أَعۡبُدُ مَا تَعۡبُدُونَ

2. "I worship not that which you worship,

Verse 109:3 Focus Verse →

وَلَآ أَنتُمۡ عَٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعۡبُدُ

3. "Nor will you worship that which I worship.

Verse 109:4 Focus Verse →

وَلَآ أَنَا۠ عَابِدٞ مَّا عَبَدتُّمۡ

4. "And I shall not worship that which you are worshipping.

Verse 109:5 Focus Verse →

وَلَآ أَنتُمۡ عَٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعۡبُدُ

5. "Nor will you worship that which I worship.

Verse 109:6 Focus Verse →

لَكُمۡ دِينُكُمۡ وَلِيَ دِينِ

6. "To you be your religion, and to me my religion (Islâmic Monotheism)."
