Al-Anfal

(Verse 14)

Al-Anfal (8:14)

ذَٰلِكُمۡ فَذُوقُوهُ وَأَنَّ لِلۡكَٰفِرِينَ عَذَابَ ٱلنَّارِ

This is (the torment), so taste it; and surely for the disbelievers is the torment of the Fire.
