(Verse 51)

Al-Anfal (8:51)

ذَٰلِكَ بِمَا قَدَّمَتۡ أَيۡدِيكُمۡ وَأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَيۡسَ بِظَلَّٰمٖ لِّلۡعَبِيدِ

51. "This is because of that which your hands had forwarded. And verily, Allâh is not unjust to His slaves."
