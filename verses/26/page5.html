Ash-Shu'ara'

(227 Verses)

Page 5 of 8

26:121

إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَأٓيَةٗۖ وَمَا كَانَ أَكۡثَرُهُم مُّؤۡمِنِينَ

Verily, in this is indeed a sign, yet most of them are not believers.

26:122

وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ

And verily your Lord, He is indeed the All-Mighty, the Most Merciful.

26:123

كَذَّبَتۡ عَادٌ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ

‘Âd (people) belied the Messengers.

26:124

إِذۡ قَالَ لَهُمۡ أَخُوهُمۡ هُودٌ أَلَا تَتَّقُونَ

When their brother Hûd said to them: "Will you not fear Allâh and obey Him?

26:125

إِنِّي لَكُمۡ رَسُولٌ أَمِينٞ

"Verily I am a trustworthy Messenger to you.

26:126

فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ

"So fear Allâh, keep your duty to Him, and obey me.

26:127

وَمَآ أَسۡـَٔلُكُمۡ عَلَيۡهِ مِنۡ أَجۡرٍۖ إِنۡ أَجۡرِيَ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَٰلَمِينَ

"No reward do I ask of you for it (my Message of Islâmic Monotheism); my reward is only from the Lord of the ‘Âlamîn (mankind, jinn, and all that exists).

26:128

أَتَبۡنُونَ بِكُلِّ رِيعٍ ءَايَةٗ تَعۡبَثُونَ

"Do you build high palaces on every high place, while you do not live in them?

26:129

وَتَتَّخِذُونَ مَصَانِعَ لَعَلَّكُمۡ تَخۡلُدُونَ

"And do you get for yourselves palaces (fine buildings) as if you will live therein for ever.

Footnote — Verse 26:129

[2] (V.26:129) See the footnote (C) of (V.9:111).
26:130

وَإِذَا بَطَشۡتُم بَطَشۡتُمۡ جَبَّارِينَ

"And when you seize (somebody), seize you (him) as tyrants?

26:131

فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ

"So fear Allâh, keep your duty to Him, and obey me.

26:132

وَٱتَّقُواْ ٱلَّذِيٓ أَمَدَّكُم بِمَا تَعۡلَمُونَ

"And keep your duty to Him, fear Him Who has aided you with all (good things) that you know.

26:133

أَمَدَّكُم بِأَنۡعَٰمٖ وَبَنِينَ

"He has aided you with cattle and children.

26:134

وَجَنَّٰتٖ وَعُيُونٍ

"And gardens and springs.

26:135

إِنِّيٓ أَخَافُ عَلَيۡكُمۡ عَذَابَ يَوۡمٍ عَظِيمٖ

"Verily, I fear for you the torment of a Great Day."

26:136

قَالُواْ سَوَآءٌ عَلَيۡنَآ أَوَعَظۡتَ أَمۡ لَمۡ تَكُن مِّنَ ٱلۡوَٰعِظِينَ

They said: "It is the same to us whether you preach or be not of those who preach.

26:137

إِنۡ هَٰذَآ إِلَّا خُلُقُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ

"This is no other than the false tales and religion of the ancients, [Tafsir At-Tabarî]

26:138

وَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمُعَذَّبِينَ

"And we are not going to be punished."

26:139

فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَأَهۡلَكۡنَٰهُمۡۚ إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَأٓيَةٗۖ وَمَا كَانَ أَكۡثَرُهُم مُّؤۡمِنِينَ

So they belied him, and We destroyed them. Verily in this is indeed a sign, yet most of them are not believers.

26:140

وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ

And verily your Lord, He is indeed the All-Mighty, the Most Merciful.

26:141

كَذَّبَتۡ ثَمُودُ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ

Thamûd (people) belied the Messengers.

26:142

إِذۡ قَالَ لَهُمۡ أَخُوهُمۡ صَٰلِحٌ أَلَا تَتَّقُونَ

When their brother Sâlih said to them: "Will you not fear Allâh and obey Him?

26:143

إِنِّي لَكُمۡ رَسُولٌ أَمِينٞ

"I am a trustworthy Messenger to you.

26:144

فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ

"So fear Allâh, keep your duty to Him, and obey me.

26:145

وَمَآ أَسۡـَٔلُكُمۡ عَلَيۡهِ مِنۡ أَجۡرٍۖ إِنۡ أَجۡرِيَ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَٰلَمِينَ

"No reward do I ask of you for it (my Message of Islâmic Monotheism); my reward is only from the Lord of the ‘Âlamîn (mankind, jinn and all that exists).

26:146

أَتُتۡرَكُونَ فِي مَا هَٰهُنَآ ءَامِنِينَ

"Will you be left secure in that which you have here?

26:147

فِي جَنَّٰتٖ وَعُيُونٖ

"In gardens and springs.

26:148

وَزُرُوعٖ وَنَخۡلٖ طَلۡعُهَا هَضِيمٞ

And green crops (fields) and date-palms with soft spadix.

26:149

وَتَنۡحِتُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡجِبَالِ بُيُوتٗا فَٰرِهِينَ

"And you hew out in the mountains, houses with great skill.

26:150

فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ

"So fear Allâh, keep your duty to Him, and obey me.
