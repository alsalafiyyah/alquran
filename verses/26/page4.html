Ash-Shu'ara'

(227 Verses)

Page 4 of 8

26:91

وَبُرِّزَتِ ٱلۡجَحِيمُ لِلۡغَاوِينَ ۝91

And the (Hell) Fire will be placed in full view of the erring.

26:92

وَقِيلَ لَهُمۡ أَيۡنَ مَا كُنتُمۡ تَعۡبُدُونَ ۝92

And it will be said to them: "Where are those (the false gods whom you used to set up as rivals with Allâh) that you used to worship.

26:93

مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ هَلۡ يَنصُرُونَكُمۡ أَوۡ يَنتَصِرُونَ ۝93

"Instead of Allâh? Can they help you or (even) help themselves?"

26:94

فَكُبۡكِبُواْ فِيهَا هُمۡ وَٱلۡغَاوُۥنَ ۝94

Then they will be thrown on their faces into the (Fire), they and the Ghâwûn (devils, and those who were in error).

26:95

وَجُنُودُ إِبۡلِيسَ أَجۡمَعُونَ ۝95

And the whole hosts of Iblîs (Satan) together.

26:96

قَالُواْ وَهُمۡ فِيهَا يَخۡتَصِمُونَ ۝96

They will say while contending therein,

26:97

تَٱللَّهِ إِن كُنَّا لَفِي ضَلَٰلٖ مُّبِينٍ ۝97

By Allâh, we were truly in a manifest error,

26:98

إِذۡ نُسَوِّيكُم بِرَبِّ ٱلۡعَٰلَمِينَ ۝98

When We held you (false gods) as equals (in worship) with the Lord of the ‘Âlamîn (mankind, jinn and all that exists);

26:99

وَمَآ أَضَلَّنَآ إِلَّا ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُونَ ۝99

And none has brought us into error except the Mujrimûn [Iblîs (Satan) and those of human beings who commit crimes, murderers, polytheists, oppressors].

26:100

فَمَا لَنَا مِن شَٰفِعِينَ ۝100

Now we have no intercessors,

26:101

وَلَا صَدِيقٍ حَمِيمٖ ۝101

Nor a close friend (to help us).

26:102

فَلَوۡ أَنَّ لَنَا كَرَّةٗ فَنَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ ۝102

(Alas!) If we only had a chance to return (to the world), we shall truly be among the believers!

26:103

إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَأٓيَةٗۖ وَمَا كَانَ أَكۡثَرُهُم مُّؤۡمِنِينَ ۝103

Verily in this is indeed a sign, yet most of them are not believers.

26:104

وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ ۝104

And verily, your Lord, He is truly the All-Mighty, the Most Merciful.

26:105

كَذَّبَتۡ قَوۡمُ نُوحٍ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ ۝105

The people of Nûh (Noah) belied the Messengers.

26:106

إِذۡ قَالَ لَهُمۡ أَخُوهُمۡ نُوحٌ أَلَا تَتَّقُونَ ۝106

When their brother Nûh (Noah) said to them: "Will you not fear Allâh and obey Him?

26:107

إِنِّي لَكُمۡ رَسُولٌ أَمِينٞ ۝107

"I am a trustworthy Messenger to you.

26:108

فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ ۝108

"So fear Allâh, keep your duty to Him, and obey me.

26:109

وَمَآ أَسۡـَٔلُكُمۡ عَلَيۡهِ مِنۡ أَجۡرٍۖ إِنۡ أَجۡرِيَ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَٰلَمِينَ ۝109

"No reward do I ask of you for it (my Message of Islâmic Monotheism); my reward is only from the Lord of the ‘Âlamîn (mankind, jinn and all that exists).

26:110

فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ ۝110

"So keep your duty to Allâh, fear Him and obey me."

26:111

۞ قَالُوٓاْ أَنُؤۡمِنُ لَكَ وَٱتَّبَعَكَ ٱلۡأَرۡذَلُونَ ۝111

They said: "Shall we believe in you, when the meanest (of the people) follow you?"

26:112

قَالَ وَمَا عِلۡمِي بِمَا كَانُواْ يَعۡمَلُونَ ۝112

He said: "And what knowledge have I of what they used to do?

26:113

إِنۡ حِسَابُهُمۡ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّيۖ لَوۡ تَشۡعُرُونَ ۝113

"Their account is only with my Lord, if you could (but) know.

26:114

وَمَآ أَنَا۠ بِطَارِدِ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ ۝114

"And I am not going to drive away the believers.

26:115

إِنۡ أَنَا۠ إِلَّا نَذِيرٞ مُّبِينٞ ۝115

I am only a plain warner."

26:116

قَالُواْ لَئِن لَّمۡ تَنتَهِ يَٰنُوحُ لَتَكُونَنَّ مِنَ ٱلۡمَرۡجُومِينَ ۝116

They said: "If you cease not, O Nûh (Noah) you will surely be among those stoned (to death)."

26:117

قَالَ رَبِّ إِنَّ قَوۡمِي كَذَّبُونِ ۝117

He said: "My Lord! Verily, my people have belied me.

26:118

فَٱفۡتَحۡ بَيۡنِي وَبَيۡنَهُمۡ فَتۡحٗا وَنَجِّنِي وَمَن مَّعِيَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ ۝118

Therefore judge You between me and them, and save me and those of the believers who are with me."

26:119

فَأَنجَيۡنَٰهُ وَمَن مَّعَهُۥ فِي ٱلۡفُلۡكِ ٱلۡمَشۡحُونِ ۝119

And We saved him and those with him in the laden ship.

26:120

ثُمَّ أَغۡرَقۡنَا بَعۡدُ ٱلۡبَاقِينَ ۝120

Then We drowned the rest (disbelievers) thereafter.
