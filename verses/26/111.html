(Verse 111)

Ash-Shu'ara' (26:111)

۞ قَالُوٓاْ أَنُؤۡمِنُ لَكَ وَٱتَّبَعَكَ ٱلۡأَرۡذَلُونَ

111. They said: "Shall we believe in you, when the meanest (of the people) follow you?"
