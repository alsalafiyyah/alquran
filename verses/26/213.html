(Verse 213)

Ash-Shu'ara' (26:213)

فَلَا تَدۡعُ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَٰهًا ءَاخَرَ فَتَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُعَذَّبِينَ

213. So invoke not with Allâh another ilâh (god) lest you should be among those who receive punishment.
