(Verse 160)

Ash-Shu'ara' (26:160)

كَذَّبَتۡ قَوۡمُ لُوطٍ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ

160. The people of Lût (Lot) (-who dwelt in the town of Sodom in Palestine) belied the Messengers.
