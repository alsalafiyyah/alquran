Ash-Shu'ara'

(Verse 94)

Ash-Shu'ara' (26:94)

فَكُبۡكِبُواْ فِيهَا هُمۡ وَٱلۡغَاوُۥنَ

Then they will be thrown on their faces into the (Fire), they and the Ghâwûn (devils, and those who were in error).
