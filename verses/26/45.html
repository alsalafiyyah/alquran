(Verse 45)

Ash-Shu'ara' (26:45)

فَأَلۡقَىٰ مُوسَىٰ عَصَاهُ فَإِذَا هِيَ تَلۡقَفُ مَا يَأۡفِكُونَ

Then Mûsâ (Moses) threw his stick, and behold, it swallowed up all that they falsely showed!
