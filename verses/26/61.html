(Verse 61)

Ash-Shu'ara' (26:61)

فَلَمَّا تَرَٰٓءَا ٱلۡجَمۡعَانِ قَالَ أَصۡحَٰبُ مُوسَىٰٓ إِنَّا لَمُدۡرَكُونَ

61. And when the two hosts saw each other, the companions of Mûsâ (Moses) said: "We are sure to be overtaken."
