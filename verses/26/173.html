(Verse 173)

Ash-Shu'ara' (26:173)

وَأَمۡطَرۡنَا عَلَيۡهِم مَّطَرٗاۖ فَسَآءَ مَطَرُ ٱلۡمُنذَرِينَ

173. And We rained on them a rain (of torment). And how evil was the rain of those who had been warned!
