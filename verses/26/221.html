Ash-Shu'ara' (26:221)

هَلۡ أُنَبِّئُكُمۡ عَلَىٰ مَن تَنَزَّلُ ٱلشَّيَٰطِينُ

221. Shall I inform you (O people!) upon whom the Shayâtîn (devils) descend?
