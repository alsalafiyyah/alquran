(Verse 156)

Ash-Shu'ara' (26:156)

وَلَا تَمَسُّوهَا بِسُوٓءٖ فَيَأۡخُذَكُمۡ عَذَابُ يَوۡمٍ عَظِيمٖ

156. "And touch her not with harm, lest the torment of a Great Day should seize you."
