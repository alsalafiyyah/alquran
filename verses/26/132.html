Ash-Shu'ara' (26:132)

وَٱتَّقُواْ ٱلَّذِيٓ أَمَدَّكُم بِمَا تَعۡلَمُونَ

132. "And keep your duty to Him, fear Him Who has aided you with all (good things) that you know.
