(Verse 99)

Ash-Shu'ara' (26:99)

وَمَآ أَضَلَّنَآ إِلَّا ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُونَ

And none has brought us into error except the Mujrimûn [Iblîs (Satan) and those of human beings who commit crimes, murderers, polytheists, oppressors].
