Al-Muzzammil (73:19)

إِنَّ هَٰذِهِۦ تَذۡكِرَةٞۖ فَمَن شَآءَ ٱتَّخَذَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّهِۦ سَبِيلًا

19. Verily, this is an admonition: therefore whosoever will, let him take a Path to His Lord!
