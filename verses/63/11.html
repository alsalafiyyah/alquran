Al-Munafiqun

(Verse 11)

Al-Munafiqun (63:11)

وَلَن يُؤَخِّرَ ٱللَّهُ نَفۡسًا إِذَا جَآءَ أَجَلُهَاۚ وَٱللَّهُ خَبِيرُۢ بِمَا تَعۡمَلُونَ

And Allâh grants respite to none when his appointed time (death) comes. And Allâh is All-Aware of what you do.
