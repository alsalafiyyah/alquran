Al-Munafiqun

(Verse 3)

Al-Munafiqun (63:3)

ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمۡ ءَامَنُواْ ثُمَّ كَفَرُواْ فَطُبِعَ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِهِمۡ فَهُمۡ لَا يَفۡقَهُونَ

That is because they believed, and then disbelieved; therefore their hearts are sealed, so they understand not.
