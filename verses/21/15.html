Al-Anbiya'

(Verse 15)

Al-Anbiya' (21:15)

فَمَا زَالَت تِّلۡكَ دَعۡوَىٰهُمۡ حَتَّىٰ جَعَلۡنَٰهُمۡ حَصِيدًا خَٰمِدِينَ

And that cry of theirs ceased not, till We made them as a field that is reaped, extinct (dead).
