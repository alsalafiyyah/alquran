Al-Anbiya'

(Verse 95)

Al-Anbiya' (21:95)

وَحَرَٰمٌ عَلَىٰ قَرۡيَةٍ أَهۡلَكۡنَٰهَآ أَنَّهُمۡ لَا يَرۡجِعُونَ

And a ban is laid on every town (population) which We have destroyed that they shall not return (to this world again, nor repent to Us).
