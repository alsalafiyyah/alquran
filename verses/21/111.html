(Verse 111)

Al-Anbiya' (21:111)

وَإِنۡ أَدۡرِي لَعَلَّهُۥ فِتۡنَةٞ لَّكُمۡ وَمَتَٰعٌ إِلَىٰ حِينٖ

111. And I know not, perhaps it may be a trial for you, and an enjoyment for a while.
