Maryam

(Verse 15)

Maryam (19:15)

وَسَلَٰمٌ عَلَيۡهِ يَوۡمَ وُلِدَ وَيَوۡمَ يَمُوتُ وَيَوۡمَ يُبۡعَثُ حَيّٗا

And Salâm (peace) be on him the day he was born, and the day he dies, and the day he will be raised up to life (again)!
