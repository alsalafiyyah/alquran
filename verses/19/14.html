Maryam (19:14)

وَبَرَّۢا بِوَٰلِدَيۡهِ وَلَمۡ يَكُن جَبَّارًا عَصِيّٗا

14. And dutiful towards his parents, and he was neither arrogant nor disobedient (to Allâh or to his parents).
