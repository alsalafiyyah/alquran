19:71

وَإِن مِّنكُمۡ إِلَّا وَارِدُهَاۚ كَانَ عَلَىٰ رَبِّكَ حَتۡمٗا مَّقۡضِيّٗا

71. There is not one of you but will pass over it (Hell): this is with your Lord; a Decree which must be accomplished.[13]

[13] (V.19:71) See the footnote (C) of (V.68:42).
