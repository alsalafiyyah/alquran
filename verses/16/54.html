(Verse 54)

An-Nahl (16:54)

ثُمَّ إِذَا كَشَفَ ٱلضُّرَّ عَنكُمۡ إِذَا فَرِيقٞ مِّنكُم بِرَبِّهِمۡ يُشۡرِكُونَ

Then, when He has removed the harm from you, behold! some of you associate others in worship with their Lord (Allâh).
