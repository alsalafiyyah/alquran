An-Naml

(Verse 38)

An-Naml (27:38)

قَالَ يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلۡمَلَؤُاْ أَيُّكُمۡ يَأۡتِينِي بِعَرۡشِهَا قَبۡلَ أَن يَأۡتُونِي مُسۡلِمِينَ

He said: "O chiefs! Which of you can bring me her throne before they come to me surrendering themselves in obedience?"
