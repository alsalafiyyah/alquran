(Verse 58)

An-Naml (27:58)

وَأَمۡطَرۡنَا عَلَيۡهِم مَّطَرٗاۖ فَسَآءَ مَطَرُ ٱلۡمُنذَرِينَ

And We rained down on them a rain (of stones). So evil was the rain of those who were warned.
