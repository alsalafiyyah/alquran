(Verse 25)

Al-Mulk (67:25)

وَيَقُولُونَ مَتَىٰ هَٰذَا ٱلۡوَعۡدُ إِن كُنتُمۡ صَٰدِقِينَ

They say: "When will this promise (i.e. the Day of Resurrection) come to pass if you are telling the truth.?"
