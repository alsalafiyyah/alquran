'Abasa

(42 Verses)

Page 2 of 2

80:31

وَفَٰكِهَةٗ وَأَبّٗا ۝31

And fruits and herbage

80:32

مَّتَٰعٗا لَّكُمۡ وَلِأَنۡعَٰمِكُمۡ ۝32

(To be) a provision and benefit for you and your cattle.

80:33

فَإِذَا جَآءَتِ ٱلصَّآخَّةُ ۝33

Then when there comes As-Sâkhkhah (the second blowing of the Trumpet on the Day of Resurrection)

80:34

يَوۡمَ يَفِرُّ ٱلۡمَرۡءُ مِنۡ أَخِيهِ ۝34

That Day shall a man flee from his brother,

80:35

وَأُمِّهِۦ وَأَبِيهِ ۝35

And from his mother and his father,

80:36

وَصَٰحِبَتِهِۦ وَبَنِيهِ ۝36

And from his wife and his children.

80:37

لِكُلِّ ٱمۡرِيٕٖ مِّنۡهُمۡ يَوۡمَئِذٖ شَأۡنٞ يُغۡنِيهِ ۝37

Everyman that Day will have enough to make him careless of others.

80:38

وُجُوهٞ يَوۡمَئِذٖ مُّسۡفِرَةٞ ۝38

Some faces that Day will be bright (true believers of Islâmic Monotheism),

80:39

ضَاحِكَةٞ مُّسۡتَبۡشِرَةٞ ۝39

Laughing, rejoicing at good news (of Paradise).

80:40

وَوُجُوهٞ يَوۡمَئِذٍ عَلَيۡهَا غَبَرَةٞ ۝40

And other faces, that Day, will be dust-stained.

80:41

تَرۡهَقُهَا قَتَرَةٌ ۝41

Darkness will cover them.

80:42

أُوْلَٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلۡكَفَرَةُ ٱلۡفَجَرَةُ ۝42

Such will be the Kafarah (disbelievers in Allâh, in His Oneness, and in His Messenger Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم, etc.), the Fajarah (wicked evil doers).
