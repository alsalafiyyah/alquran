'Abasa

(42 Verses)

Page 1 of 2

80:1

عَبَسَ وَتَوَلَّىٰٓ ۝1

(The Prophet صلى الله عليه وسلم) frowned and turned away.

80:2

أَن جَآءَهُ ٱلۡأَعۡمَىٰ ۝2

Because there came to him the blind man (i.e. ‘Abdullâh bin Umm-Maktûm, who came to the Prophet صلى الله عليه وسلم while he was preaching to one or some of the Quraish chiefs).

80:3

وَمَا يُدۡرِيكَ لَعَلَّهُۥ يَزَّكَّىٰٓ ۝3

And how can you know that he might become pure (from sins)?

80:4

أَوۡ يَذَّكَّرُ فَتَنفَعَهُ ٱلذِّكۡرَىٰٓ ۝4

Or he might receive admonition, and the admonition might profit him?

80:5

أَمَّا مَنِ ٱسۡتَغۡنَىٰ ۝5

As for him who thinks himself self-sufficient,

80:6

فَأَنتَ لَهُۥ تَصَدَّىٰ ۝6

To him you attend;

80:7

وَمَا عَلَيۡكَ أَلَّا يَزَّكَّىٰ ۝7

What does it matter to you if he will not become pure (from disbelief: you are only a Messenger, your duty is to convey the Message of Allâh).

80:8

وَأَمَّا مَن جَآءَكَ يَسۡعَىٰ ۝8

But as to him who came to you running,

80:9

وَهُوَ يَخۡشَىٰ ۝9

And is afraid (of Allâh and His punishment).

80:10

فَأَنتَ عَنۡهُ تَلَهَّىٰ ۝10

Of him you are neglectful and divert your attention to another,

80:11

كَلَّآ إِنَّهَا تَذۡكِرَةٞ ۝11

Nay, (do not do like this); indeed it (this Qur’ân) is an admonition.

80:12

فَمَن شَآءَ ذَكَرَهُۥ ۝12

So whoever wills, let him pay attention to it.

80:13

فِي صُحُفٖ مُّكَرَّمَةٖ ۝13

(It is) in Records held (greatly) in honour (Al-Lauh Al-Mahfûz),

80:14

مَّرۡفُوعَةٖ مُّطَهَّرَةِۭ ۝14

Exalted (in dignity), purified,

80:15

بِأَيۡدِي سَفَرَةٖ ۝15

In the hands of scribes (angels).

80:16

كِرَامِۭ بَرَرَةٖ ۝16

Honourable and obedient.

80:17

قُتِلَ ٱلۡإِنسَٰنُ مَآ أَكۡفَرَهُۥ ۝17

Be cursed (the disbelieving) man! How ungrateful he is!

80:18

مِنۡ أَيِّ شَيۡءٍ خَلَقَهُۥ ۝18

From what thing did He create him?

80:19

مِن نُّطۡفَةٍ خَلَقَهُۥ فَقَدَّرَهُۥ ۝19

From Nutfah (male and female semen drops) He created him and then set him in due proportion.

80:20

ثُمَّ ٱلسَّبِيلَ يَسَّرَهُۥ ۝20

Then He makes the Path easy for him.

80:21

ثُمَّ أَمَاتَهُۥ فَأَقۡبَرَهُۥ ۝21

Then He causes him to die and puts him in his grave.

80:22

ثُمَّ إِذَا شَآءَ أَنشَرَهُۥ ۝22

Then when it is His Will, He will resurrect him (again).

80:23

كَلَّا لَمَّا يَقۡضِ مَآ أَمَرَهُۥ ۝23

Nay, but (man) has not done what He commanded him.

80:24

فَلۡيَنظُرِ ٱلۡإِنسَٰنُ إِلَىٰ طَعَامِهِۦٓ ۝24

Then let man look at his food:

80:25

أَنَّا صَبَبۡنَا ٱلۡمَآءَ صَبّٗا ۝25

We pour forth water in abundance.

80:26

ثُمَّ شَقَقۡنَا ٱلۡأَرۡضَ شَقّٗا ۝26

And We split the earth in clefts.

80:27

فَأَنۢبَتۡنَا فِيهَا حَبّٗا ۝27

And We cause therein the grain to grow,

80:28

وَعِنَبٗا وَقَضۡبٗا ۝28

And grapes and clover plants (i.e. green fodder for the cattle),

80:29

وَزَيۡتُونٗا وَنَخۡلٗا ۝29

And olives and date-palms,

80:30

وَحَدَآئِقَ غُلۡبٗا ۝30

And gardens dense with many trees,
