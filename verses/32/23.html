As-Sajdah (32:23)

وَلَقَدۡ ءَاتَيۡنَا مُوسَى ٱلۡكِتَٰبَ فَلَا تَكُن فِي مِرۡيَةٖ مِّن لِّقَآئِهِۦۖ وَجَعَلۡنَٰهُ هُدٗى لِّبَنِيٓ إِسۡرَٰٓءِيلَ

23. And indeed We gave Mûsa (Moses) the Scripture [the Taurât (Torah)] So be not you in doubt of meeting him [i.e. when you met Mûsa (Moses) during the night of Al-Isrâ’ and Al-Mi‘râj [4] over the heavens]. And We made it [the Taurât (Torah)] a guide to the Children of Israel.

[4] (V.32:23): [Al-Mi‘râj المعراج - See the footnote of (V.53:12), Hadîth No.429].
