Al-Ankabut

(Verse 54)

Al-Ankabut (29:54)

يَسۡتَعۡجِلُونَكَ بِٱلۡعَذَابِ وَإِنَّ جَهَنَّمَ لَمُحِيطَةُۢ بِٱلۡكَٰفِرِينَ

54. They ask you to hasten on the torment. And verily, Hell, of a surety, will encompass the disbelievers.
