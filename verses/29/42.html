(Verse 42)

Al-Ankabut (29:42)

إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَعۡلَمُ مَا يَدۡعُونَ مِن دُونِهِۦ مِن شَيۡءٖۚ وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلۡحَكِيمُ

42. Verily, Allâh knows what things they invoke instead of Him. He is the All-Mighty, the All-Wise.[7]

[7] (V.29:42) See the footnote of (V.2:165).
