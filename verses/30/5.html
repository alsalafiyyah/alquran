Ar-Rum

(Verse 5)

Ar-Rum (30:5)

بِنَصۡرِ ٱللَّهِۚ يَنصُرُ مَن يَشَآءُۖ وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ

5. With the help of Allâh. He helps whom He wills, and He is the All-Mighty, the Most Merciful.
