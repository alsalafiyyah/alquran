Ar-Rum

(Verse 34)

Ar-Rum (30:34)

لِيَكۡفُرُواْ بِمَآ ءَاتَيۡنَٰهُمۡۚ فَتَمَتَّعُواْ فَسَوۡفَ تَعۡلَمُونَ

34. So as to be ungrateful for the graces which We have bestowed on them. Then enjoy (your short life); but you will come to know.
