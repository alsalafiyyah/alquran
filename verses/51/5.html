Adh-Dhariyat

(Verse 5)

Adh-Dhariyat (51:5)

إِنَّمَا تُوعَدُونَ لَصَادِقٞ

5. Verily, that which you are promised (i.e. Resurrection in the Hereafter and receiving the reward or punishment of good or bad deeds) is surely true.
