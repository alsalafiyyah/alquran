(Verse 40)

Adh-Dhariyat (51:40)

فَأَخَذۡنَٰهُ وَجُنُودَهُۥ فَنَبَذۡنَٰهُمۡ فِي ٱلۡيَمِّ وَهُوَ مُلِيمٞ

So We took him and his hosts, and dumped them into the sea, for he was blameworthy.
