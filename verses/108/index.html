Surah 108

Verse 108:1 Focus Verse →

إِنَّآ أَعۡطَيۡنَٰكَ ٱلۡكَوۡثَرَ

1. Verily, We have granted you (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) Al-Kauthar (a river in Paradise).[1]

Verse 108:2 Focus Verse →

فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَٱنۡحَرۡ

2. Therefore turn in prayer to your Lord and sacrifice (to Him only).

Verse 108:3 Focus Verse →

إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ ٱلۡأَبۡتَرُ

3. For he who hates you (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم), he will be cut off (from posterity and every good thing in this world and in the Hereafter).[2]
