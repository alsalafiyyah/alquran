Al-Furqan

(Verse 49)

Al-Furqan (25:49)

لِّنُحۡـِۧيَ بِهِۦ بَلۡدَةٗ مَّيۡتٗا وَنُسۡقِيَهُۥ مِمَّا خَلَقۡنَآ أَنۡعَٰمٗا وَأَنَاسِيَّ كَثِيرٗا

49. That We may give life thereby to a dead land, and We give to drink thereof many of the cattle and men that We had created.
