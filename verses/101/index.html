Surah 101

Verse 101:1 Focus Verse →

ٱلۡقَارِعَةُ

1. Al-Qâri‘ah (the striking Hour i.e. the Day of Resurrection).

Verse 101:2 Focus Verse →

مَا ٱلۡقَارِعَةُ

2. What is the striking (Hour)?

Verse 101:3 Focus Verse →

وَمَآ أَدۡرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلۡقَارِعَةُ

3. And what will make you know what the striking (Hour) is?

Verse 101:4 Focus Verse →

يَوۡمَ يَكُونُ ٱلنَّاسُ كَٱلۡفَرَاشِ ٱلۡمَبۡثُوثِ

4. It is a Day whereon mankind will be like moths scattered about.

Verse 101:5 Focus Verse →

وَتَكُونُ ٱلۡجِبَالُ كَٱلۡعِهۡنِ ٱلۡمَنفُوشِ

5. And the mountains will be like carded wool.

Verse 101:6 Focus Verse →

فَأَمَّا مَن ثَقُلَتۡ مَوَٰزِينُهُۥ

6. Then as for him whose balance (of good deeds) will be heavy,[1]

Verse 101:7 Focus Verse →

فَهُوَ فِي عِيشَةٖ رَّاضِيَةٖ

7. He will live a pleasant life (in Paradise).

Verse 101:8 Focus Verse →

وَأَمَّا مَنۡ خَفَّتۡ مَوَٰزِينُهُۥ

8. But as for him whose balance (of good deeds) will be light,

Verse 101:9 Focus Verse →

فَأُمُّهُۥ هَاوِيَةٞ

9. He will have his home in Hâwiyah (pit, i.e. Hell).

Verse 101:10 Focus Verse →

وَمَآ أَدۡرَىٰكَ مَا هِيَهۡ

10. And what will make you know what it is?

Verse 101:11 Focus Verse →

نَارٌ حَامِيَةُۢ

11. (It is) a fiercely blazing Fire!
