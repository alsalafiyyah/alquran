Al-Insan

(Verse 6)

Al-Insan (76:6)

عَيۡنٗا يَشۡرَبُ بِهَا عِبَادُ ٱللَّهِ يُفَجِّرُونَهَا تَفۡجِيرٗا

6. A spring wherefrom the slaves of Allâh will drink, causing it to gush forth abundantly.
