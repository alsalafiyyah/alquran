Al-Insan

(Verse 27)

Al-Insan (76:27)

إِنَّ هَٰٓؤُلَآءِ يُحِبُّونَ ٱلۡعَاجِلَةَ وَيَذَرُونَ وَرَآءَهُمۡ يَوۡمٗا ثَقِيلٗا

Verily, these (disbelievers) love the present life of this world, and put behind them a heavy Day (that will be hard).
