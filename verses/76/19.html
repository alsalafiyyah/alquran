Al-Insan

(Verse 19)

Al-Insan (76:19)

۞ وَيَطُوفُ عَلَيۡهِمۡ وِلۡدَٰنٞ مُّخَلَّدُونَ إِذَا رَأَيۡتَهُمۡ حَسِبۡتَهُمۡ لُؤۡلُؤٗا مَّنثُورٗا

19. And round about them will (serve) boys of everlasting youth. If you see them, you would think them scattered pearls.
