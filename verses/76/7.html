Al-Insan

(Verse 7)

Al-Insan (76:7)

يُوفُونَ بِٱلنَّذۡرِ وَيَخَافُونَ يَوۡمٗا كَانَ شَرُّهُۥ مُسۡتَطِيرٗا

7. They (are those who) fulfil (their) vows, and they fear a Day whose evil will be wide-spreading.
