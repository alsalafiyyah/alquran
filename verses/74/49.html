Al-Muddaththir

(Verse 49)

Al-Muddaththir (74:49)

فَمَا لَهُمۡ عَنِ ٱلتَّذۡكِرَةِ مُعۡرِضِينَ

Then what is wrong with them (i.e. the polytheists, the disbelievers) that they turn away from (receiving) admonition?
