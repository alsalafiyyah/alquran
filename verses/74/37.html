Al-Muddaththir

(Verse 37)

Al-Muddaththir (74:37)

لِمَن شَآءَ مِنكُمۡ أَن يَتَقَدَّمَ أَوۡ يَتَأَخَّرَ

To any of you that chooses to go forward (by working righteous deeds), or to remain behind (by committing sins).
