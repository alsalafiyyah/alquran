Al-Muddaththir

(Verse 35)

Al-Muddaththir (74:35)

إِنَّهَا لَإِحۡدَى ٱلۡكُبَرِ

35. Verily, it (Hell, or their denial of Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم, or the Day of Resurrection) is but one of the greatest (signs).
