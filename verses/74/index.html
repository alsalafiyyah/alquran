Al-Muddaththir

(56 Verses)

Page 1 of 2

74:1

يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلۡمُدَّثِّرُ ۝1

O you (Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) enveloped in garments!

74:2

قُمۡ فَأَنذِرۡ ۝2

Arise and warn!

74:3

وَرَبَّكَ فَكَبِّرۡ ۝3

And magnify your Lord (Allâh)!

74:4

وَثِيَابَكَ فَطَهِّرۡ ۝4

And purify your garments!

74:5

وَٱلرُّجۡزَ فَٱهۡجُرۡ ۝5

And keep away from Ar-Rujz (the idols)!

74:6

وَلَا تَمۡنُن تَسۡتَكۡثِرُ ۝6

And give not a thing in order to have more (or consider not your deeds of obedience to Allâh as a favour to Him).

74:7

وَلِرَبِّكَ فَٱصۡبِرۡ ۝7

And be patient for the sake of your Lord (i.e. perform your duty to Allâh)!

74:8

فَإِذَا نُقِرَ فِي ٱلنَّاقُورِ ۝8

Then, when the Trumpet is sounded (i.e. the second blowing of the horn).

74:9

فَذَٰلِكَ يَوۡمَئِذٖ يَوۡمٌ عَسِيرٌ ۝9

Truly, that Day will be a Hard Day -

74:10

عَلَى ٱلۡكَٰفِرِينَ غَيۡرُ يَسِيرٖ ۝10

Far from easy for the disbelievers.

74:11

ذَرۡنِي وَمَنۡ خَلَقۡتُ وَحِيدٗا ۝11

Leave Me Alone (to deal) with whom I created lonely (without any wealth or children etc, i.e. Al-Walîd bin Al-Mughîrah Al-Makhzûmî)!

74:12

وَجَعَلۡتُ لَهُۥ مَالٗا مَّمۡدُودٗا ۝12

And then granted him resources in abundance.

74:13

وَبَنِينَ شُهُودٗا ۝13

And children to be by his side.

74:14

وَمَهَّدتُّ لَهُۥ تَمۡهِيدٗا ۝14

And made life smooth and comfortable for him.

74:15

ثُمَّ يَطۡمَعُ أَنۡ أَزِيدَ ۝15

After all that he desires that I should give more;

74:16

كَلَّآۖ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ لِأٓيَٰتِنَا عَنِيدٗا ۝16

Nay! Verily, he has been opposing Our Ayât (proofs, evidence, verses, lessons, signs, revelations).

74:17

سَأُرۡهِقُهُۥ صَعُودًا ۝17

I shall oblige him to (climb a slippery mountain in the Hell-fire called As-Sa‘ûd, or) face a severe torment!

74:18

إِنَّهُۥ فَكَّرَ وَقَدَّرَ ۝18

Verily, he thought and plotted.

74:19

فَقُتِلَ كَيۡفَ قَدَّرَ ۝19

So let him be cursed: how he plotted!

74:20

ثُمَّ قُتِلَ كَيۡفَ قَدَّرَ ۝20

And once more let him be cursed: how he plotted!

74:21

ثُمَّ نَظَرَ ۝21

Then he thought.

74:22

ثُمَّ عَبَسَ وَبَسَرَ ۝22

Then he frowned and he looked in a bad tempered way;

74:23

ثُمَّ أَدۡبَرَ وَٱسۡتَكۡبَرَ ۝23

Then he turned back, and was proud.

74:24

فَقَالَ إِنۡ هَٰذَآ إِلَّا سِحۡرٞ يُؤۡثَرُ ۝24

Then he said: "This is nothing but magic from that of old,

74:25

إِنۡ هَٰذَآ إِلَّا قَوۡلُ ٱلۡبَشَرِ ۝25

"This is nothing but the word of a human being!"

74:26

سَأُصۡلِيهِ سَقَرَ ۝26

I will cast him into Hell-fire.

Footnote — Verse 74:26

[1] (V.74:26) See the footnote of (V.17:97).
74:27

وَمَآ أَدۡرَىٰكَ مَا سَقَرُ ۝27

And what will make you know (exactly) what Hell-fire is?

74:28

لَا تُبۡقِي وَلَا تَذَرُ ۝28

It spares not (any sinner), nor does it leave (anything unburnt)!

74:29

لَوَّاحَةٞ لِّلۡبَشَرِ ۝29

Burning and blackening the skins!

74:30

عَلَيۡهَا تِسۡعَةَ عَشَرَ ۝30

Over it are nineteen (angels as guardians and keepers of Hell).
