An-Naba'

(40 Verses)

Page 1 of 2

78:1

عَمَّ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ ۝1

What are they asking (one another) about?

78:2

عَنِ ٱلنَّبَإِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ ۝2

About the great news, (i.e. Islâmic Monotheism, the Qur’ân, which Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم brought and the Day of Resurrection)

78:3

ٱلَّذِي هُمۡ فِيهِ مُخۡتَلِفُونَ ۝3

About which they are in disagreement.

78:4

كَلَّا سَيَعۡلَمُونَ ۝4

Nay, they will come to know!

78:5

ثُمَّ كَلَّا سَيَعۡلَمُونَ ۝5

Nay, again, they will come to know!

78:6

أَلَمۡ نَجۡعَلِ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ مِهَٰدٗا ۝6

Have We not made the earth as a bed,

78:7

وَٱلۡجِبَالَ أَوۡتَادٗا ۝7

And the mountains as pegs?

78:8

وَخَلَقۡنَٰكُمۡ أَزۡوَٰجٗا ۝8

And We have created you in pairs (male and female, tall and short, good and bad, etc.).

78:9

وَجَعَلۡنَا نَوۡمَكُمۡ سُبَاتٗا ۝9

And We have made your sleep as a thing for rest.

78:10

وَجَعَلۡنَا ٱلَّيۡلَ لِبَاسٗا ۝10

And We have made the night as a covering (through its darkness),

78:11

وَجَعَلۡنَا ٱلنَّهَارَ مَعَاشٗا ۝11

And We have made the day for livelihood.

78:12

وَبَنَيۡنَا فَوۡقَكُمۡ سَبۡعٗا شِدَادٗا ۝12

And We have built above you seven strong (heavens),

78:13

وَجَعَلۡنَا سِرَاجٗا وَهَّاجٗا ۝13

And We have made (therein) a shining lamp (sun).

78:14

وَأَنزَلۡنَا مِنَ ٱلۡمُعۡصِرَٰتِ مَآءٗ ثَجَّاجٗا ۝14

And We have sent down from the rainy clouds abundant water.

78:15

لِّنُخۡرِجَ بِهِۦ حَبّٗا وَنَبَاتٗا ۝15

That We may produce therewith corn and vegetations,

78:16

وَجَنَّٰتٍ أَلۡفَافًا ۝16

And gardens of thick growth.

78:17

إِنَّ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡفَصۡلِ كَانَ مِيقَٰتٗا ۝17

Verily, the Day of Decision is a fixed time,

78:18

يَوۡمَ يُنفَخُ فِي ٱلصُّورِ فَتَأۡتُونَ أَفۡوَاجٗا ۝18

The Day when the Trumpet will be blown, and you shall come forth in crowds (groups after groups). [Tafsir At-Tabari]

78:19

وَفُتِحَتِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ فَكَانَتۡ أَبۡوَٰبٗا ۝19

And the heaven shall be opened, and it will become as gates,

78:20

وَسُيِّرَتِ ٱلۡجِبَالُ فَكَانَتۡ سَرَابًا ۝20

And the mountains shall be moved away from their places and they will be as if they were a mirage.

78:21

إِنَّ جَهَنَّمَ كَانَتۡ مِرۡصَادٗا ۝21

Truly, Hell is a place of ambush -

78:22

لِّلطَّٰغِينَ مَـَٔابٗا ۝22

A dwelling place for the Tâghûn (those who transgress the boundary limits set by Allâh like polytheists, disbelievers in the Oneness of Allâh, hyprocrites, sinners, criminals),

78:23

لَّٰبِثِينَ فِيهَآ أَحۡقَابٗا ۝23

They will abide therein for ages.

78:24

لَّا يَذُوقُونَ فِيهَا بَرۡدٗا وَلَا شَرَابًا ۝24

Nothing cool shall they taste therein, nor any drink.

78:25

إِلَّا حَمِيمٗا وَغَسَّاقٗا ۝25

Except boiling water, and dirty wound discharges -

78:26

جَزَآءٗ وِفَاقًا ۝26

An exact recompense (according to their evil crimes).

78:27

إِنَّهُمۡ كَانُواْ لَا يَرۡجُونَ حِسَابٗا ۝27

For verily, they used not to look for a reckoning.

78:28

وَكَذَّبُواْ بِـَٔايَٰتِنَا كِذَّابٗا ۝28

But they belied Our Ayât (proofs, evidence, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, and that which Our Prophet صلى الله عليه وسلم brought) completely.

78:29

وَكُلَّ شَيۡءٍ أَحۡصَيۡنَٰهُ كِتَٰبٗا ۝29

And all things We have recorded in a Book.

78:30

فَذُوقُواْ فَلَن نَّزِيدَكُمۡ إِلَّا عَذَابًا ۝30

So taste you (the results of your evil actions). No increase shall We give you, except in torment.
