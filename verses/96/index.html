Surah 96

Verse 96:1 Focus Verse →

ٱقۡرَأۡ بِٱسۡمِ رَبِّكَ ٱلَّذِي خَلَقَ

1. Read! In the Name of your Lord Who has created (all that exists).

Verse 96:2 Focus Verse →

خَلَقَ ٱلۡإِنسَٰنَ مِنۡ عَلَقٍ

2. He has created man from a clot (a piece of thick coagulated blood).

Verse 96:3 Focus Verse →

ٱقۡرَأۡ وَرَبُّكَ ٱلۡأَكۡرَمُ

3. Read! And your Lord is the Most Generous.

Verse 96:4 Focus Verse →

ٱلَّذِي عَلَّمَ بِٱلۡقَلَمِ

4. Who has taught (the writing) by the pen.

Verse 96:5 Focus Verse →

عَلَّمَ ٱلۡإِنسَٰنَ مَا لَمۡ يَعۡلَمۡ

5. He has taught man that which he knew not.

Verse 96:6 Focus Verse →

كَلَّآ إِنَّ ٱلۡإِنسَٰنَ لَيَطۡغَىٰٓ

6. Nay! Verily, man does transgress (in disbelief and evil deed).

Verse 96:7 Focus Verse →

أَن رَّءَاهُ ٱسۡتَغۡنَىٰٓ

7. Because he considers himself self-sufficient.

Verse 96:8 Focus Verse →

إِنَّ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ ٱلرُّجۡعَىٰٓ

8. Surely, unto your Lord is the return.

Verse 96:9 Focus Verse →

أَرَءَيۡتَ ٱلَّذِي يَنۡهَىٰ

9. Have you (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) seen him (i.e. Abû Jahl) who prevents

Verse 96:10 Focus Verse →

عَبۡدًا إِذَا صَلَّىٰٓ

10. A slave (Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) when he prays?

Verse 96:11 Focus Verse →

أَرَءَيۡتَ إِن كَانَ عَلَى ٱلۡهُدَىٰٓ

11. Tell me if he (Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) is on the guidance (of Allâh)

Verse 96:12 Focus Verse →

أَوۡ أَمَرَ بِٱلتَّقۡوَىٰٓ

12. Or enjoins piety!

Verse 96:13 Focus Verse →

أَرَءَيۡتَ إِن كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّىٰٓ

13. Tell me if he (Abû Jahl) denies (the truth, i.e. this Qur’ân) and turns away?

Verse 96:14 Focus Verse →

أَلَمۡ يَعۡلَم بِأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَرَىٰ

14. Knows he not that Allâh does see (what he does)?

Verse 96:15 Focus Verse →

كَلَّا لَئِن لَّمۡ يَنتَهِ لَنَسۡفَعَۢا بِٱلنَّاصِيَةِ

15. Nay! If he (Abû Jahl) ceases not, We will catch him by the forelock -

Verse 96:16 Focus Verse →

نَاصِيَةٖ كَٰذِبَةٍ خَاطِئَةٖ

16. A lying, sinful forelock!

Verse 96:17 Focus Verse →

فَلۡيَدۡعُ نَادِيَهُۥ

17. Then let him call upon his council (of helpers).

Verse 96:18 Focus Verse →

سَنَدۡعُ ٱلزَّبَانِيَةَ

18. We will call out the guards of Hell (to deal with him)!

Verse 96:19 Focus Verse →

كَلَّا لَا تُطِعۡهُ وَٱسۡجُدۡۤ وَٱقۡتَرِب۩

19. Nay! (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم)! Do not obey him (Abû Jahl). Fall prostrate and draw near to Allâh!
