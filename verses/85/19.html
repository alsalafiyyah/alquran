Al-Buruj

(Verse 19)

Al-Buruj (85:19)

بَلِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ فِي تَكۡذِيبٖ

Nay! The disbelievers (persisted) in denying (Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم and his Message of Islâmic Monotheism).
