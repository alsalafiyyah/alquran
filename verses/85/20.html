Al-Buruj

(Verse 20)

Al-Buruj (85:20)

وَٱللَّهُ مِن وَرَآئِهِم مُّحِيطُۢ

20. And Allâh encompasses them from behind! (i.e. all their deeds are within His Knowledge, and He will requite them for their deeds).
