(Verse 6)

Az-Zalzalah (99:6)

يَوۡمَئِذٖ يَصۡدُرُ ٱلنَّاسُ أَشۡتَاتٗا لِّيُرَوۡاْ أَعۡمَٰلَهُمۡ

That Day mankind will proceed in scattered groups that they may be shown their deeds.
