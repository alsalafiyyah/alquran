20:41

وَٱصۡطَنَعۡتُكَ لِنَفۡسِي

41. "And I have chosen you,[3] for Myself.

[3] (V.20:41) i.e. chosen you for My Revelation and My Message or created you for Myself or strengthened and taught you as to how to preach My Message to My worshippers. (Tafsîr Al-Qurtubî)
