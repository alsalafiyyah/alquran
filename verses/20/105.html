Ta-Ha (20:105)

وَيَسۡـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلۡجِبَالِ فَقُلۡ يَنسِفُهَا رَبِّي نَسۡفٗا

105. And they ask you concerning the mountains: say, "My Lord will blast them and scatter them as particles of dust.
