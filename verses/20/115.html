(Verse 115)

Ta-Ha (20:115)

وَلَقَدۡ عَهِدۡنَآ إِلَىٰٓ ءَادَمَ مِن قَبۡلُ فَنَسِيَ وَلَمۡ نَجِدۡ لَهُۥ عَزۡمٗا

115. And indeed We made a covenant with Adam before, but he forgot, and We found on his part no firm will-power.
