(Verse 103)

Ta-Ha (20:103)

يَتَخَٰفَتُونَ بَيۡنَهُمۡ إِن لَّبِثۡتُمۡ إِلَّا عَشۡرٗا

103. They will speak in a very low voice to each other (saying): "You stayed not longer than ten (days)."
