(Verse 84)

Ta-Ha (20:84)

قَالَ هُمۡ أُوْلَآءِ عَلَىٰٓ أَثَرِي وَعَجِلۡتُ إِلَيۡكَ رَبِّ لِتَرۡضَىٰ

84. He said: "They are close on my footsteps: and I hastened to You, O my Lord, that You might be pleased."
