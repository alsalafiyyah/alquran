Verse 70:31 Focus Verse →

فَمَنِ ٱبۡتَغَىٰ وَرَآءَ ذَٰلِكَ فَأُوْلَٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلۡعَادُونَ

31. But whosoever seeks beyond that, then it is those who are trespassers.[5]

Verse 70:32 Focus Verse →

وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ لِأَمَٰنَٰتِهِمۡ وَعَهۡدِهِمۡ رَٰعُونَ

32. And those who keep their trusts and covenants.

Verse 70:33 Focus Verse →

وَٱلَّذِينَ هُم بِشَهَٰدَٰتِهِمۡ قَآئِمُونَ

33. And those who stand firm in their testimonies.

Verse 70:34 Focus Verse →

وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ عَلَىٰ صَلَاتِهِمۡ يُحَافِظُونَ

34. And those who guard their Salât (prayers) well.[6]

Verse 70:35 Focus Verse →

أُوْلَٰٓئِكَ فِي جَنَّٰتٖ مُّكۡرَمُونَ

35. Such shall dwell in the Gardens (i.e. Paradise), honoured.

Verse 70:36 Focus Verse →

فَمَالِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ قِبَلَكَ مُهۡطِعِينَ

36. So what is the matter with those who disbelieve that they hasten to listen from you [O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم), in order to belie you and to mock at you, and at Allâh’s Book (this Qur’ân)].

Verse 70:37 Focus Verse →

عَنِ ٱلۡيَمِينِ وَعَنِ ٱلشِّمَالِ عِزِينَ

37. (Sitting) in groups on the right and on the left (of you, O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم)?

Verse 70:38 Focus Verse →

أَيَطۡمَعُ كُلُّ ٱمۡرِيٕٖ مِّنۡهُمۡ أَن يُدۡخَلَ جَنَّةَ نَعِيمٖ

38. Does every man of them hope to enter the Paradise of Delight?

Verse 70:39 Focus Verse →

كَلَّآۖ إِنَّا خَلَقۡنَٰهُم مِّمَّا يَعۡلَمُونَ

39. No, that is not like that! Verily, We have created them out of that which they know!

Verse 70:40 Focus Verse →

فَلَآ أُقۡسِمُ بِرَبِّ ٱلۡمَشَٰرِقِ وَٱلۡمَغَٰرِبِ إِنَّا لَقَٰدِرُونَ

40. So I swear by the Lord of all the [three hundred and sixty-five (365)] points of sunrise and sunset in the east and the west that surely We are Able

Verse 70:41 Focus Verse →

عَلَىٰٓ أَن نُّبَدِّلَ خَيۡرٗا مِّنۡهُمۡ وَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمَسۡبُوقِينَ

41. To replace them by (others) better than them; and We are not to be outrun.

Verse 70:42 Focus Verse →

فَذَرۡهُمۡ يَخُوضُواْ وَيَلۡعَبُواْ حَتَّىٰ يُلَٰقُواْ يَوۡمَهُمُ ٱلَّذِي يُوعَدُونَ

42. So leave them to plunge in vain talk[7] and play about, until they meet their Day which they are promised -

Verse 70:43 Focus Verse →

يَوۡمَ يَخۡرُجُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡأَجۡدَاثِ سِرَاعٗا كَأَنَّهُمۡ إِلَىٰ نُصُبٖ يُوفِضُونَ

43. The Day when they will come out of the graves quickly as racing to a goal,

Verse 70:44 Focus Verse →

خَٰشِعَةً أَبۡصَٰرُهُمۡ تَرۡهَقُهُمۡ ذِلَّةٞۚ ذَٰلِكَ ٱلۡيَوۡمُ ٱلَّذِي كَانُواْ يُوعَدُونَ

44. With their eyes lowered in fear and humility, ignominy covering them (all over)! That is the Day which they were promised!
