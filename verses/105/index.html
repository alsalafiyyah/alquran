Surah 105

Verse 105:1 Focus Verse →

أَلَمۡ تَرَ كَيۡفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِأَصۡحَٰبِ ٱلۡفِيلِ

1. Have you (O Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) not seen how your Lord dealt with the owners of the Elephant? [The Elephant army which came from Yemen under the command of Abrahah Al-Ashram intending to destroy the Ka‘bah at Makkah].

Verse 105:2 Focus Verse →

أَلَمۡ يَجۡعَلۡ كَيۡدَهُمۡ فِي تَضۡلِيلٖ

2. Did He not make their plot go astray?

Verse 105:3 Focus Verse →

وَأَرۡسَلَ عَلَيۡهِمۡ طَيۡرًا أَبَابِيلَ

3. And He sent against them birds, in flocks,

Verse 105:4 Focus Verse →

تَرۡمِيهِم بِحِجَارَةٖ مِّن سِجِّيلٖ

4. Striking them with stones of Sijjîl (baked clay).

Verse 105:5 Focus Verse →

فَجَعَلَهُمۡ كَعَصۡفٖ مَّأۡكُولِۭ

5. And He made them like (an empty field of) stalks (of which the corn has been eaten up by cattle).[1]
