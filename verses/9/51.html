(Verse 51)

At-Tawbah (9:51)

قُل لَّن يُصِيبَنَآ إِلَّا مَا كَتَبَ ٱللَّهُ لَنَا هُوَ مَوۡلَىٰنَاۚ وَعَلَى ٱللَّهِ فَلۡيَتَوَكَّلِ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنُونَ

51. Say: "Nothing shall ever happen to us except what Allâh has ordained for us.[13] He is our Maulâ (Lord, Helper and Protector)." And in Allâh let the believers put their trust.

[13] (V.9:51) See the footnote of (V.57:22).
