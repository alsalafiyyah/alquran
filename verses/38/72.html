Sad (38:72)

فَإِذَا سَوَّيۡتُهُۥ وَنَفَخۡتُ فِيهِ مِن رُّوحِي فَقَعُواْ لَهُۥ سَٰجِدِينَ

72. So when I have fashioned him and breathed into him (his) soul created by Me, then you fall down prostrate to him."
