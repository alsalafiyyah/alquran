Sad (38:85)

لَأَمۡلَأَنَّ جَهَنَّمَ مِنكَ وَمِمَّن تَبِعَكَ مِنۡهُمۡ أَجۡمَعِينَ

That I will fill Hell with you [Iblîs (Satan)] and those of them (mankind) that follow you, together."
