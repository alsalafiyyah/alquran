Al-Qiyamah

(Verse 29)

Al-Qiyamah (75:29)

وَٱلۡتَفَّتِ ٱلسَّاقُ بِٱلسَّاقِ

And one leg will be joined with another leg (shrouded).

Footnote — Verse 75:29

[3] (V.75:29) Or it may mean: hardship and distress will be joined with another hardship and distress (i.e. distress of death, and of the thought as to what is going to happen to him in the Hereafter). (Tafsîr At-Tabarî ).
