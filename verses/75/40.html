Al-Qiyamah

(Verse 40)

Al-Qiyamah (75:40)

أَلَيۡسَ ذَٰلِكَ بِقَٰدِرٍ عَلَىٰٓ أَن يُحۡـِۧيَ ٱلۡمَوۡتَىٰ

40. Is not He (Allâh Who does that) Able to give life to the dead? (Yes! He is Able to do all things).
