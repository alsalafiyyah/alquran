Ash-Sharh

(Verse 6)

Ash-Sharh (94:6)

إِنَّ مَعَ ٱلۡعُسۡرِ يُسۡرٗا

Verily, along with every hardship is relief (i.e. there is one hardship with two reliefs, so one hardship cannot overcome two reliefs).
