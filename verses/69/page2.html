Surah 69

Page 2 of 2

Verse 69:31 Focus Verse →

ثُمَّ ٱلۡجَحِيمَ صَلُّوهُ

31. Then throw him in the blazing Fire.

Verse 69:32 Focus Verse →

ثُمَّ فِي سِلۡسِلَةٖ ذَرۡعُهَا سَبۡعُونَ ذِرَاعٗا فَٱسۡلُكُوهُ

32. "Then fasten him with a chain whereof the length is seventy cubits!"

Verse 69:33 Focus Verse →

إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ لَا يُؤۡمِنُ بِٱللَّهِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ

33. Verily, he used not to believe in Allâh, the Most Great,

Verse 69:34 Focus Verse →

وَلَا يَحُضُّ عَلَىٰ طَعَامِ ٱلۡمِسۡكِينِ

34. And urged not on the feeding of Al-Miskîn (the poor).[1]

Verse 69:35 Focus Verse →

فَلَيۡسَ لَهُ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ هَٰهُنَا حَمِيمٞ

35. So no friend has he here this Day,

Verse 69:36 Focus Verse →

وَلَا طَعَامٌ إِلَّا مِنۡ غِسۡلِينٖ

36. Nor any food except filth from the washing of wounds.

Verse 69:37 Focus Verse →

لَّا يَأۡكُلُهُۥٓ إِلَّا ٱلۡخَٰطِـُٔونَ

37. None will eat it except the Khâti’ûn (sinners, disbelievers, polytheists).

Verse 69:38 Focus Verse →

فَلَآ أُقۡسِمُ بِمَا تُبۡصِرُونَ

38. So I swear by whatsoever you see,

Verse 69:39 Focus Verse →

وَمَا لَا تُبۡصِرُونَ

39. And by whatsoever you see not,

Verse 69:40 Focus Verse →

إِنَّهُۥ لَقَوۡلُ رَسُولٖ كَرِيمٖ

40. That this is verily, the word of an honoured Messenger [i.e. Jibrîl (Gabriel) or Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم which he has brought from Allâh].

Verse 69:41 Focus Verse →

وَمَا هُوَ بِقَوۡلِ شَاعِرٖۚ قَلِيلٗا مَّا تُؤۡمِنُونَ

41. It is not the word of a poet: little is that you believe!

Verse 69:42 Focus Verse →

وَلَا بِقَوۡلِ كَاهِنٖۚ قَلِيلٗا مَّا تَذَكَّرُونَ

42. Nor is it the word of a soothsayer (or a foreteller): little is that you remember!

Verse 69:43 Focus Verse →

تَنزِيلٞ مِّن رَّبِّ ٱلۡعَٰلَمِينَ

43. This is the Revelation sent down from the Lord of the ‘Âlamîn (mankind, jinn and all that exists).

Verse 69:44 Focus Verse →

وَلَوۡ تَقَوَّلَ عَلَيۡنَا بَعۡضَ ٱلۡأَقَاوِيلِ

44. And if he (Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم) had forged a false saying concerning Us (Allâh جل جلاله),

Verse 69:45 Focus Verse →

لَأَخَذۡنَا مِنۡهُ بِٱلۡيَمِينِ

45. We surely would have seized him by his right hand (or with power and might),

Verse 69:46 Focus Verse →

ثُمَّ لَقَطَعۡنَا مِنۡهُ ٱلۡوَتِينَ

46. And then We certainly would have cut off his life artery (aorta),

Verse 69:47 Focus Verse →

فَمَا مِنكُم مِّنۡ أَحَدٍ عَنۡهُ حَٰجِزِينَ

47. And none of you could have withheld Us from (punishing) him.

Verse 69:48 Focus Verse →

وَإِنَّهُۥ لَتَذۡكِرَةٞ لِّلۡمُتَّقِينَ

48. And verily, this (Qur’an) is a Reminder for the Muttaqûn (the pious. See V.2:2).

Verse 69:49 Focus Verse →

وَإِنَّا لَنَعۡلَمُ أَنَّ مِنكُم مُّكَذِّبِينَ

49. And verily, We know that there are some among you that belie (this Qur’ân). (Tafsir At-Tabarî)

Verse 69:50 Focus Verse →

وَإِنَّهُۥ لَحَسۡرَةٌ عَلَى ٱلۡكَٰفِرِينَ

50. And indeed it (this Qur’ân) will be an anguish for the disbelievers (on the Day of Resurrection).[2]

Verse 69:51 Focus Verse →

وَإِنَّهُۥ لَحَقُّ ٱلۡيَقِينِ

51. And verily, it (this Qur’ân) is an absolute truth with certainty.[3]

Verse 69:52 Focus Verse →

فَسَبِّحۡ بِٱسۡمِ رَبِّكَ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ

52. So glorify the Name of your Lord, the Most Great.[4]
