(Verse 14)

Al-Haqqah (69:14)

وَحُمِلَتِ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ وَٱلۡجِبَالُ فَدُكَّتَا دَكَّةٗ وَٰحِدَةٗ

14. And the earth and the mountains shall be removed from their places, and crushed with a single crushing.
