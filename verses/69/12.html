Al-Haqqah

(Verse 12)

Al-Haqqah (69:12)

لِنَجۡعَلَهَا لَكُمۡ تَذۡكِرَةٗ وَتَعِيَهَآ أُذُنٞ وَٰعِيَةٞ

12. That We might make it (Noah’s ship) an admonition for you and that it might be retained by the retaining ears.
