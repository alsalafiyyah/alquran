(Verse 17)

Al-Haqqah (69:17)

وَٱلۡمَلَكُ عَلَىٰٓ أَرۡجَآئِهَاۚ وَيَحۡمِلُ عَرۡشَ رَبِّكَ فَوۡقَهُمۡ يَوۡمَئِذٖ ثَمَٰنِيَةٞ

17. And the angels will be on its sides, and eight angels will, that Day, bear the Throne of your Lord above them.
