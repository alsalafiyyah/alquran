(Verse 16)

Al-Haqqah (69:16)

وَٱنشَقَّتِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ فَهِيَ يَوۡمَئِذٖ وَاهِيَةٞ

16. And the heaven will be rent asunder, for that Day it (the heaven) will be frail and torn up.
