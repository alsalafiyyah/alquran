Luqman

(Verse 24)

Luqman (31:24)

نُمَتِّعُهُمۡ قَلِيلٗا ثُمَّ نَضۡطَرُّهُمۡ إِلَىٰ عَذَابٍ غَلِيظٖ

24. We let them enjoy for a little while, then in the end We shall oblige them to (enter) a great torment.
