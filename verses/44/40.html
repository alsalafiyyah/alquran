Ad-Dukhan (44:40)

إِنَّ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡفَصۡلِ مِيقَٰتُهُمۡ أَجۡمَعِينَ

Verily, the Day of Judgement (when Allâh will judge between the creatures) is the time appointed for all of them -
