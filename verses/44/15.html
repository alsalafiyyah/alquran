(Verse 15)

Ad-Dukhan (44:15)

إِنَّا كَاشِفُواْ ٱلۡعَذَابِ قَلِيلًاۚ إِنَّكُمۡ عَآئِدُونَ

Verily, We shall remove the torment for a while. Verily you will revert (to disbelief).
