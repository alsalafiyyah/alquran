(Verse 38)

Ad-Dukhan (44:38)

وَمَا خَلَقۡنَا ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ وَمَا بَيۡنَهُمَا لَٰعِبِينَ

And We created not the heavens and the earth, and all that is between them, for mere play.
